# Detailed Comparison

## 1. Midjourney

* * The AI Master of Art * *

Midjourney currently has the strongest artistic sense among AI image generators. Its generated images often flood social media.

* * AI Capabilities * *:
- Precise text understanding, low barrier for prompts
- Diverse art styles, from realistic to abstract
- Active community for reference

* * Pros * *:
- Strongest artistic quality
- Simple operation, easy Discord interface
- Mature community ecosystem

* * Cons * *:
- Requires VPN
- Need to pay after free credits run out
- Limited Chinese prompt support

* * Best For * *: Designers, artists, people pursuing artistic expression

---

## 2. DALL-E 3

* * The Most Understanding AI Artist * *

From OpenAI, DALL-E 3 has the strongest understanding ability, especially good at complex descriptions.

* * AI Capabilities * *:
- Precise understanding of complex descriptions
- Strong text generation, can create clear text in images
- Deep integration with ChatGPT

* * Pros * *:
- Best understanding, more detailed descriptions = better results
- Can generate images with text
- Great user experience with ChatGPT integration

* * Cons * *:
- Requires ChatGPT Plus subscription
- Less artistic than Midjourney
- Average generation speed

* * Best For * *: Content creators, people needing image-text combinations

---

## 3. Stable Diffusion

* * Tech Enthusiasts' Favorite * *

Open source and free, with local deployment, Stable Diffusion is the choice for programmers and tech enthusiasts.

* * AI Capabilities * *:
- Completely open source and free
- Supports local deployment, privacy protected
- Rich plugins, highly customizable

* * Pros * *:
- Totally free
- Local deployment keeps data private
- Rich plugin ecosystem

* * Cons * *:
- Requires GPU
- Higher learning curve
- Need to learn prompt techniques

* * Best For * *: Developers, tech enthusiasts, privacy-conscious users

---

## 4. Adobe Firefly

* * The Commercial Safety Choice * *

From Adobe, Firefly focuses on commercial safety and legitimate licensing - the choice for enterprise users.

* * AI Capabilities * *:
- Commercial copyright safe
- Deep integration with Adobe suite
- Supports generative fill, expand images

* * Pros * *:
- No copyright worries
- Suitable for commercial use
- Seamless integration with PS, AI

* * Cons * *:
- Relatively fewer features
- Requires Adobe subscription
- Conservative art styles

* * Best For * *: Enterprise users, commercial designers, those needing legitimate licensing

---

## 5. Leonardo AI

* * The Feature-Rich Contender * *

A rising star with rich features and free credits, good value for money.

* * AI Capabilities * *:
- Multiple pre-trained models
- Supports AI canvas, real-time generation
- Active community

* * Pros * *:
- Generous free credits
- Rich features
- User-friendly interface

* * Cons * *:
- Unstable access in China
- Free version has generation limits
- Less artistic than Midjourney

* * Best For * *: Designers, creative professionals, budget-conscious users

# Advanced Tips

## Tip 1: Use in Combination

Use different tools for different scenarios:

- Use Midjourney for inspiration in creative phase
- Use DALL-E 3 to refine after determining the approach
- Use Stable Diffusion for batch generation

## Tip 2: Prompt Optimization

Good prompts are half the battle:

```
Subject description + Style keywords + Lighting + Color + Composition
```

For example:
```
A cat wearing a suit, working in an office, cinematic lighting, warm colors, rule of thirds
```

## Tip 3: Leverage Community Resources

Both Midjourney and Leonardo have active communities. Reference others' prompts and parameters to quickly improve your results.
