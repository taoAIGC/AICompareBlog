## Let's Cut to the Chase

There's no standard answer to this question.

It's like asking "Mercedes or BMW?" — it depends on what you need.

Each AI has its strengths:

**Coding: Claude wins**
**Writing: ChatGPT is solid**
**Chinese reasoning: DeepSeek offers the best value**

But that's just my experience.

You need to consider your specific use case.

## Let's Talk About Each One

### ChatGPT

OpenAI's flagship product.

The first to go mainstream and blow up everywhere.

GPT-4 is currently the most well-rounded option.

Writing, coding, translating, analyzing — it does it all.

The downside? You need a VPN to use it, and it costs money (Plus is $20/month).

Highest barrier for Chinese users.

### Claude

Made by Anthropic.

Founders were ex-OpenAI employees.

Strengths: long-form text processing and logical reasoning.

Amazing for coding — detailed comments and clean structure.

Large context window, can handle super long documents.

Decent Chinese performance, but not as natural as ChatGPT.

Free version is sufficient, Pro version is affordable.

### DeepSeek

The dark horse among Chinese LLMs.

V2 version got everyone talking.

Exceptional Chinese capabilities — great for web writing and marketing copy.

Coding ability is catching up quickly.

Best of all: free or extremely cheap.

No VPN needed — biggest advantage.

## Real-World Comparison

### Scenario 1: Writing Code

I asked all three AIs to write a Python scraper.

**ChatGPT**: Decent code, runs fine, but comments are average.

**Claude**: Well-structured code, detailed comments, handles edge cases.

**DeepSeek**: Efficient and concise code, but error handling isn't as robust.

Verdict: Claude for quality, DeepSeek for speed, ChatGPT for reliability.

### Scenario 2: Writing Marketing Copy

Write a Little Red Book post promoting Bluetooth earbuds.

**ChatGPT**: Professional-sounding, has that "pro marketer" vibe.

**Claude**: More human, like a friend recommending something, pleasant to read.

**DeepSeek**: Most authentic Chinese internet slang, younger audiences will love it.

Verdict: Choose based on your brand tone. Claude for premium, DeepSeek for trendy, ChatGPT for safe.

### Scenario 3: Writing Articles

A 2000-word AI tools comparison article.

**ChatGPT**: Most complete structure, covers everything, but feels a bit formulaic.

**Claude**: Deeper analysis, unique insights, but can be wordy.

**DeepSeek**: Rich examples and素材, but logic can be jumpy.

Verdict: Claude for depth, DeepSeek for speed, ChatGPT for safety.

### Scenario 4: Translation

Translate a product description from English.

**ChatGPT**: Most fluent, reads like a native speaker wrote it.

**Claude**: Accurate, but occasionally has a translation feel.

**DeepSeek**: Natural and casual, sometimes too informal.

Verdict: ChatGPT for formal documents, DeepSeek for casual content.

## Pricing Comparison

### ChatGPT

- Free version: Usable but slow, rate-limited
- Plus: $20/month, unlimited GPT-4
- Team: $25/month per person

### Claude

- Free version: Sufficient for most people
- Pro: $20/month
- Long context, great for long documents

### DeepSeek

- Free version: Just use it
- API: Extremely cheap
- Best value for money

If you're just playing around, DeepSeek is the most cost-effective.

If you need productivity, paid ChatGPT or Claude is worth it.

## Barrier to Entry

### ChatGPT

Most troublesome.

Needs VPN, needs phone number, needs credit card.

Highest barrier for mainland users.

### Claude

Relatively easier.

Still needs VPN.

Registration is a bit simpler than ChatGPT.

### DeepSeek

Easiest of all.

Web version directly, no VPN needed, no registration required (for now).

Most user-friendly for Chinese users.
