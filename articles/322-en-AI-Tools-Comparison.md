## Mainstream AI Tools Comparison

### 1. DeepSeek

**Access**: chat.deepseek.com

**My Experience**:
- Biggest surprise of 2024 among domestic AIs
- Top-tier Chinese language capability, no translation feel
- Free quota is basically unlimited
- Reasoning model R1 is free - huge win

**Pros**:
- First-tier Chinese capability
- Completely free
- Stable access in China
- Affordable API pricing

**Cons**:
- English writing can be Chinglish
- Slightly weak in creativity

**Best For**:
- Chinese Q&A and writing
- Programming questions
- Budget-conscious users

---

### 2. Kimi

**Access**: kimi.moonshot.cn

**My Experience**:
- From Moonshot, truly good
- Insane long-text processing, supports 2 million characters
- Generous free quota
- Clean interface, no ads

**Pros**:
- Strong long-text understanding
- Natural Chinese expression
- Supports file upload and reading
- Fast response

**Cons**:
- Occasionally unresponsive
- Average creativity

**Best For**:
- Document summarization and Q&A
- Long article writing
- Data organization
