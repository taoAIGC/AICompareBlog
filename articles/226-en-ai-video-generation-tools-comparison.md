## Why Are AI Video Tools So Hot Lately?

Have you noticed that more and more movie-level videos on your social media are actually AI-generated?

This isn't an illusion. AI video generation technology is evolving at an amazing speed.

A year ago, AI-generated videos were just a few seconds long, with often jittery and distorted visuals. But now? You can generate smooth videos lasting several seconds with just a single prompt, and the results are hard to distinguish from reality.

Today I'll help you compare the mainstream AI video generation tools to see which one better suits your needs.

## Detailed Comparison

### 1. Runway - The Professional's Choice

If you need to create truly usable commercial videos, Runway is currently the most mature choice.

**Pros**:
- Most comprehensive features: text-to-video, image-to-video, video extension, style transfer
- Stable video quality, high success rate
- Supports 4K output
- Professional post-production tools

**Cons**:
- Steeper learning curve
- Free version has usage limits
- Relatively slower generation speed

**Best for**: Professional video creators, small advertising agencies

### 2. Pika - The Best Choice for Beginners

If you just want to post fun AI videos on social media, Pika is definitely worth trying.

**Pros**:
- Extremely simple operation, just type text
- Fast generation speed
- Often delivers surprising results
- Free version is sufficient

**Cons**:
- Limited video duration
- Limited complex scene handling
- Paid version required for commercial use

**Best for**: Social media influencers, regular users

### 3. Luma - The Visual Quality Favorite

Luma is currently among the top tier in terms of visual quality.

**Pros**:
- Detailed, realistic visuals
- Smooth and natural motion
- Good understanding of Chinese prompts

**Cons**:
- Slower generation speed
- Sometimes produces strange character movements
- Higher paid pricing

**Best for**: Creators who prioritize visual quality

### 4. Sora - The Future Potential

OpenAI's Sora isn't fully open yet, but has already shown amazing capabilities.

**Pros**:
- Can generate videos up to 1 minute long
- Strong understanding of the physical world
- Most realistic visual effects

**Cons**:
- Not yet widely available
- Requires application and waiting
- Pricing unknown

**Best for**: Forward-thinking users following AI development

## My Personal Usage Suggestions

### Choose Based on Scenario

- **Posting on TikTok/YouTube Shorts**: Pika is most convenient, can produce one in minutes
- **Making ads/client work**: Runway is more professional, can meet client requirements
- **Creative experiments**: Luma's visual quality is worth trying
- **Early positioning**: Keep an eye on Sora, apply when possible

### Combined Use Works Better

My personal habit is: use Pika to quickly create creative prototypes, then use Runway for fine-tuning.

This maintains both creative efficiency and final quality.
