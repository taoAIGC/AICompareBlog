## 怎么选？

**选 Gamma：**
- Pursuit of speed and appearance- Need to publish quickly- There are requirements for Chinese typesetting
**选 Beautiful.ai：**
- Professional business style required- High requirements on chart quality- Willing to take the time to fine-tune
**选 Canva：**
- Design novice- Requires a lot of materials- Pursue simplicity and ease of use

## 省钱小技巧

Most tools have free versions:
- **Gamma**: Free version generates 10 for every 900 credits- **Beautiful.ai**: The free version can only create 3 PPTs- **Tome**: Free version with a maximum of 10 pages per project- **Canva**: Free version is enough for daily use
Heavy users are recommended to pay, and the price of the professional version starts from $12/month.
---

## 我的选择

I currently use **Gamma**, mainly to save time.
The PPT that used to take 6 hours to complete can now be done in 30 minutes. Wouldn’t it be nice to spend the rest of the time with your wife and children?
Of course, if you have particularly high design requirements, you still have to adjust it manually. AI is just the starting point, not the end.
---

## 彩蛋

During the test, I also found that the content generated by these AI PPT tools sometimes has "hallucinations" - that is, it compiles some data that seems reasonable but does not actually exist.
Therefore, you must review it yourself before important presentations. AI is responsible for rapid generation, and you are responsible for ensuring accuracy.
