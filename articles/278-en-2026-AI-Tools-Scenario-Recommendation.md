## Introduction

Too many AI tools, it's normal to feel overwhelmed.

Someone asked me: I want to make short videos, which AI tool should I use? I want to write code, which AI coding tool is best?

Actually there's no best AI tool, only the most suitable tool for the current scenario. Today I'll recommend the most suitable AI tools based on different use scenarios.

---

## Scenario 1: Writing/Content Creation

**Recommended Tools**:
- **Daily Writing**: Kimi (strong long-text processing)
- **Text Editing**: Meta Writing Cat (professional proofreading)
- **Creative Writing**: Claude (most creative)

**Experience**:
Kimi is great for long articles, can handle hundreds of thousands of characters at once; Meta Writing Cat specializes in proofreading, essential for writers; Claude has the most creativity for content needing inspiration.

---

## Scenario 2: Coding/Programmers

**Recommended Tools**:
- **Daily Coding**: DeepSeek Coder (free & fast)
- **Complex Projects**: Claude (strongest code ability)
- **Enterprise**: Tongyi Lingma (Alibaba ecosystem)

**Experience**:
DeepSeek Coder is completely free, fast response, good code quality; Claude handles complex logic most stably; Tongyi Lingma works well with Alibaba products.

---

## Scenario 3: Image/Design

**Recommended Tools**:
- **Daily Images**: Tongyi Wanxiang (fast & high quality)
- **Chinese Style**: Wenxin Yige (best Chinese style)
- **Professional Design**: Midjourney (best results)

**Experience**:
Tongyi Wanxiang is fast, usually satisfied after a few tries; Wenxin Yige is perfect for Chinese style elements; Midjourney has unbeatable results but requires payment.

---

## Scenario 4: Video/Content Creator

**Recommended Tools**:
- **Quick Production**: Jimeng (ByteDance, fast)
- **Long Videos**: Kling (feature-rich)
- **Translation/Dubbing**: Hailuo (accurate translation)

**Experience**:
Jimeng is super convenient for short videos, can produce content after a few tries; Kling has more features but slower generation; Hailuo's translation and dubbing are excellent.

---

## Scenario 5: PPT/Office Workers

**Recommended Tools**:
- **Workplace Reports**: iFlytek Smart Doc (many templates)
- **Academic Presentations**: Meta PPT (rigorous style)
- **Quick Drafts**: AiPPT (fastest)

**Experience**:
iFlytek Smart Doc has comprehensive workplace templates; Meta PPT has rigorous style for academic use; AiPPT is fastest for tight deadlines.

---

## Scenario 6: Learning/Students

**Recommended Tools**:
- **Research**: DeepSeek (free)
- **Q&A**: Kimi (patient)
- **Thesis Writing**: Kimi (strong long-text)

**Experience**:
Students save money with DeepSeek free tier; Kimi is very patient answering questions; great for thesis writing with dozens of pages.

---

## Scenario 7: Office/Business

**Recommended Tools**:
- **Meeting Notes**: iFlytek Listening (strong speech-to-text)
- **Document Processing**: Tencent Doc AI (good collaboration)
- **Email Writing**: Wenxin Yiyan (strong Chinese)

**Experience**:
iFlytek converts meeting recordings to text directly; Tencent Doc AI has powerful collaboration features; Wenxin Yiyan writes very natural Chinese emails.

## My Advice

AI tools aren't about being expensive or having many options.

The key is finding the right tool for the scenario:
- Daily use: Choose free ones (DeepSeek, Tongyi Wanxiang)
- Professional needs: Choose strongest (Claude, Midjourney)
- Work scenarios: Choose matching ecosystem (Alibaba, Baidu)

My principle: Just enough is fine, saving money is earning money.
