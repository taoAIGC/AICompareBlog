## Important Notes

### ❌ Don't Do This

1. **Don't upload sensitive data directly** - Desensitize sensitive data before giving to AI
2. **Don't completely rely on AI judgments** - AI analysis is for reference, key decisions need human oversight
3. **Don't ignore data accuracy** - AI might misunderstand data, verify carefully
4. **Don't ignore business context** - Data analysis should consider business context, AI doesn't know your specifics

### ✅ Should Do This

1. **Prepare data in advance** - Cleaner data = more accurate analysis
2. **Be clear about analysis goals** - Tell AI what problem you're solving for better results
3. **Cross-verify results** - Double-check AI's analysis to ensure accuracy
4. **Combine with business thinking** - Combine AI's analysis with your business experience

## Common Questions

### Q1: Is AI data analysis reliable?
A: AI analysis helps quickly understand data and find patterns, but key judgments should combine with business experience. AI is an aid, not a replacement.

### Q2: How to solve data security issues?
A: Desensitize sensitive data first (remove names, ID numbers, etc.), or use locally deployed AI tools. Important data should be pre-processed by yourself.

### Q3: Can't remember Excel formulas what to do?
A: Just ask AI! Describe your need, like "I want to calculate the average of each row" - AI will write the formula for you.

### Q4: How much data can AI handle?
A: General AI tools handle a few thousand rows fine. For very large data, use Python or databases.
