- Read very long documents

## How to Register and Use?

### Step 1: Register

1. Visit Kimi website: kimi.moonshot.cn
2. Click "Start Using"
3. Register with phone number, verify with SMS
4. No authentication needed, you can use it directly

### Step 2: Start Chatting

After registration, just type in the input box. Supports:
- Text input
- File upload (PDF, Word, TXT, images, etc.)
- Send links

**When to use**:
- Reading papers
- Reading reports
- Reading contracts
- Reading long documents from colleagues

**How to use**:
Drag files to the input box, or copy-paste content, then ask what you want to know.

**Actual test**:

## Common Beginner Questions

### Q1: Is the free version enough?
A: Totally enough. The free version supports 2 million character context, enough for most documents. Paid version mainly has higher limits.

### Q2: Is there a file size limit?
A: Under 100MB per file, up to 50 files at once. Enough for regular users.

### Q3: What if responses are slow?
A: Might be slow during peak hours, avoid morning and evening peak times.

### Q4: What if answers are inaccurate?
A: Try rephrasing with more context. The more specific the question, the more accurate the answer.

## Usage Tips

### Tip 1: Give AI a role

**Wrong**: "Write something for me"
**Correct**: "You are an HR with 10 years experience, help me optimize this resume"

### Tip 2: Process in steps

**Wrong**: "Analyze all problems with this company"
**Correct**: First ask "What's this company's business model", then "What competition does this company face"

### Tip 3: Provide examples

**Wrong**: "Write in this format"
**Correct**: "Write three sentences in this format: [paste example]"

### Tip 4: Specify format

**Wrong**: "Summarize this"
**Correct**: "Summarize the pros and cons of these three plans in table format"
