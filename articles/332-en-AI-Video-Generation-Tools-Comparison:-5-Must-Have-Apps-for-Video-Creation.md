# Detailed Comparison

## 1. Runway

* * Professional AI Video Leader * *

Runway is the pioneer in AI video, with leading technology and professional results.

* * AI Capabilities * *:
- Text to video, image to video
- Video editing, extension, frame interpolation
- Style transfer, effect generation

* * Pros * *:
- Industry-leading quality
- Comprehensive professional features
- Supports 4K resolution

* * Cons * *:
- Higher learning curve
- Limited free credits
- Unstable access in China

* * Best For * *: Professional creators, advertising teams, film production

---

## 2. Pika

* * Short Video King * *

Pika focuses on short video generation and is loved by social media creators.

* * AI Capabilities * *:
- Text to video
- Image to video
- Video extension

* * Pros * *:
- Fast generation speed
- Easy to use
- Social media friendly

* * Cons * *:
- Duration limits
- Average quality for complex scenes
- Watermark on free version

* * Best For * *: Short video bloggers, social media managers, individual creators

---

## 3. Luma Dream Machine

* * Consistent Output Choice * *

Luma is a rising star, excelling in quality and consistency.

* * AI Capabilities * *:
- Text to video
- Image to video
- Video quality enhancement

* * Pros * *:
- Stable and reliable results
- Smooth natural motion
- Multi-platform export support

* * Cons * *:
- Slower generation speed
- Limited free credits
- Relatively single-function

* * Best For * *: Commercial content creators, teams needing consistent output

---

## 4. Kling AI

* * Top Choice for Chinese Users * *

Kling AI is a domestic AI video tool launched by Kuaishou, very friendly for Chinese users.

* * AI Capabilities * *:
- Text to video
- Image to video
- Video extension

* * Pros * *:
- Chinese interface, smooth operation
- Fast domestic access
- Generous free credits

* * Cons * *:
- Limited international recognition
- Some styles not as good as foreign tools
- Advanced features require payment

* * Best For * *: Domestic creators, e-commerce operators, short video beginners

---

## 5. Hailuo AI

* * New Domestic Force * *

Hailuo AI is ByteDance's AI video tool, focused on short video generation.

* * AI Capabilities * *:
- Fast short video generation
- Multiple style templates
- Smart music matching

* * Pros * *:
- Extremely fast generation
- Rich template variety
- Free to use

* * Cons * *:
- Limited video length
- Precision needs improvement
- Features still iterating

* * Best For * *: Individual users, daily short videos, social media
