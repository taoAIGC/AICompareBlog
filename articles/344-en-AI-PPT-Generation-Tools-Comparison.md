# Detailed Comparison

## 1. Gamma

* * Design King * *

The hottest AI PPT tool in recent years.

* * Core Features * *:
- AI one-click complete PPT generation
- Rich template library
- Online presentation mode
- Team collaboration

* * Pros * *:
- The design quality is really strong, not those tacky template styles
- Just input the topic and outline, AI handles content and layout
- Presentation mode is impressive, great for client meetings
- Supports PDF and PPT export

* * Cons * *:
- Requires VPN, unstable access in China
- Free version has usage limits
- Chinese generation slightly weaker than English
- Average handling of complex charts

* * Best For * *: Business professionals, entrepreneurs, those needing high-quality presentations, design-conscious users

---

## 2. Tome

* * Storytelling Pro * *

AI presentation tool known for narrative capabilities.

* * Core Features * *:
- AI-driven content generation
- Beautiful template designs
- Multimedia embedding
- Responsive layouts

* * Pros * *:
- Templates are beautifully designed, every slide looks polished
- Perfect for presentations requiring narrative logic
- Can embed videos, images, web content for richer presentations
- Generated PPTs look very professional

* * Cons * *:
- Requires VPN, inconvenient for China users
- Fewer templates compared to Gamma
- Average Chinese support
- Limited export features

* * Best For * *: Designers, product managers, storytellers, creative workers

---

## 3. 讯飞智文 (iFlytek Document)

* * Domestic Favorite * *

AI PPT tool from iFlytek, blessing for China users.

* * Core Features * *:
- AI smart generation
- Outline one-click generation
- Multiple style templates
- Best Chinese optimization

* * Pros * *:
- Strongest Chinese generation, works great with Chinese topics
- No VPN needed, stable access in China
- Free tier is enough for basic use
- Supports multiple export formats

* * Cons * *:
- Design slightly weaker than Gamma/Tome
- Template styles are more traditional
- Limited support for complex presentations
- International version experience is average

* * Best For * *: China users, Chinese presentations, daily office scenarios, budget-conscious users

---

## 4. Beautiful.ai

* * Business Professional * *

Professional tool focused on business presentations, Fortune 500 choice.

* * Core Features * *:
- Smart template system
- Enterprise brand management
- Team collaboration
- Advanced animations

* * Pros * *:
- Very professional templates, full of business feel
- Complete enterprise features, brand management and team collaboration
- Rich animation effects, more dynamic presentations
- High-quality exported files

* * Cons * *:
- Requires payment, free version very limited
- Requires VPN
- Learning curve exists
- AI generation not as good as Gamma

* * Best For * *: Enterprise users, business professionals, those needing professional presentations, teams with brand requirements

---

## 5. Presentations.ai

* * Enterprise Powerhouse * *

AI presentation tool for enterprise users, most feature-complete.

* * Core Features * *:
- AI full-process generation
- Enterprise-grade security
- Customizable templates
- Data visualization

* * Pros * *:
- Most comprehensive features, handles generation to presentation
- Enterprise-grade security, good data protection
- Strong template customization
- Good data chart generation

* * Cons * *:
- Relatively high price
- Requires VPN
- Relatively complex interface

* * Best For * *: Large enterprises, trainers, users needing many presentations, users with data visualization needs

# Real Experience Summary

## 🎯 Usage Scenarios

| Scenario | Recommended | Reason |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| High-quality business | Gamma | Best design, high client satisfaction |
| Narrative presentations | Tome | Strong storytelling, beautiful templates |
| China daily office | 讯飞智文 | No VPN, good Chinese, free tier enough |
| Business proposals | Beautiful.ai | Professional templates, business feel |
| Enterprise use | Presentations.ai | Full features, enterprise security |

## 💰 Budget Options

- **Free first**: 讯飞智文 > Gamma(free) > Tome(free)
- **Professional business**: Beautiful.ai > Presentations.ai > Gamma

## 🧪 Practical Tips

1. **Know your needs**: Emergency use or long-term? Personal or enterprise?
2. **China users first**: 讯飞智文, no VPN is key
3. **Design-focused choose Gamma**: Generated PPTs really look good
4. **Don't rely entirely on AI**: Adjust content and logic after AI generation
