## 5 AI Search Tools Deep Test

### 1. Kimi (China's Rising Star)

**Entry**: kimi.moonshot.cn

**Test Results**:
- Represents domestic AI search
- Supports long text analysis, up to 500,000 characters at once
- Search results come with source links
- Fast response, strong Chinese understanding
- Free quota: About 100 queries/day, basically enough

**Pros**:
- Strong Chinese understanding
- Long text processing
- Completely free
- Stable access in China

**Cons**:
- Occasionally "confidently incorrect"
- No multi-language search support

**Best For**:
- Chinese content search
- Long text summarization
- Daily Q&A

**Price**: Free

---

### 2. Meta AI Search (Academic Essential)

**Entry**: metaso.cn

**Test Results**:
- Clean interface, no ads
- Specifically optimized for academic search
- Shows information sources in results
- Supports both web and academic search modes
- Free quota: 50 searches/day, enough for daily use

**Pros**:
- Strong academic search
- No ad disturbance
- Clear citation sources

**Cons**:
- Sometimes answers are too brief
- Paid version has poor value

**Best For**:
- Academic material search
- Thesis-related research
- In-depth investigation

**Price**: Free version sufficient, Paid 29 RMB/month

---

### 3. TianGong AI Search (All-Rounder)

**Entry**: tiangong.cn

**Test Results**:
- From Kunlun Tech, strong technical foundation
- Supports multi-round dialogue follow-up
- Results organized in outline format
- Fast response, smooth experience
- Free quota: 100 searches/day

**Pros**:
- Clear outline organization
- Supports follow-up questions
- Generous free quota

**Cons**:
- Relatively low brand awareness
- Average in some professional areas

**Best For**:
- Daily search
- Quick understanding of new fields
- Work material search

**Price**: Free

---

### 4. 360 AI Search (Veteran's New Experience)

**Entry**: so.com/ai

**Test Results**:
- 360's AI search tool
- Inherits search engine expertise
- Results show sources and timestamps
- Very fast response
- Free quota: Unlimited

**Pros**:
- Completely free, unlimited
- Fast response
- 360 ecosystem integration

**Cons**:
- AI capability relatively weaker
- Ads still exist

**Best For**:
- Simple question search
- Quick information retrieval
- Scenarios needing fast response

**Price**: Free

---

### 5. iAsk AI (English Expert)

**Entry**: iask.ai

**Test Results**:
- Excellent English search experience
- Answers are concise and clear
- No registration required
- 50 free searches daily
- Supports multiple languages

**Pros**:
- Strong English search
- Use without registration
- Fast response

**Cons**:
- Average Chinese search
- Unstable access in China

**Best For**:
- English material search
- Programming problem lookup
- International news

**Price**: Free 50/day, Paid $5/month
