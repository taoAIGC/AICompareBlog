## 5 Popular AI Video Generator Tools Tested

I personally tested these tools, here's my real experience:

### 1. Runway — For Pro Users

**My experience**:
- Leader in AI video
- Powerful features, multiple generation modes
- Suitable for professional creators

**Pros**:
- High generation quality
- Supports text-to-video, image-to-video
- Active community, many tutorials

**Cons**:
- Pricey
- Learning curve
- Unstable access in China

**Best for**: Professional video creation, advertising, film post-production

**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Tips to Avoid Pitfalls

1. **AI video is still early**: Quality gap compared to real filming
2. **Copyright issues**: Check material rights for commercial use
3. **Generate multiple times**: Create several and pick the best
4. **Combine with manual work**: Important videos still need human editing
