## Free vs Paid - How Big Is the Gap?

Many people ask me: Is the paid version worth it?

My answer: **For most people, the free version is enough.**

But consider paid if you:
1. **Need to process long texts** - Free versions have length limits, paid can handle tens of thousands of words
2. **Need high-frequency usage** - Free versions have usage limits
3. **Need more stable responses** - Free versions may queue during peak times
