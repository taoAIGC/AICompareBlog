## Traditional vs AI Method

### Traditional Method (3+ hours)

1. Baidu search "XX industry status" → Full screen ads
2. Click through articles one by one → Head spinning
3. Copy-paste to document → Messy format
4. Organize and summarize → 2 hours
5. Write report → Another 1 hour

**Result**: Stay up late, quality might still be poor.

### AI Method (1 hour)

1. Ask 4 AIs different angle questions simultaneously → 15 min
2. AI automatically organizes key points → 10 min
3. Manual selection and stitching → 15 min
4. Format adjustment → 20 min

**Result**: Leave on time, report quality isn't bad either.

## Practical Steps

### Step 1: Clarify Research Needs

When boss says "industry research," you need to figure out exactly what they want.

**Magic phrases**:
> "Boss, I want to confirm—what aspect should I focus on? Market size, competition landscape, development trends, or a specific company?"

Two benefits:
1. Seems professional and serious
2. Clear direction, less wasted work

### Step 2: Ask Multiple AIs Simultaneously

Open your AI tool, input 4 angle questions at once:

**Question 1 (Market Size)**:
> Please introduce China's XX industry market size, growth rate, and development trends in the past 3 years.

**Question 2 (Competition Landscape)**:
> Who are the main players in China's XX industry? How is market share distributed?

**Question 3 (Development Trends)**:
> What new technologies, trends, and opportunities will the XX industry have in the next 3-5 years?

**Question 4 (Challenges & Risks)**:
> What challenges and risks does the XX industry currently face? Any policy impacts?

**Why ask 4 AIs?**
Each AI has different strengths. ChatGPT is comprehensive, Claude is logical, DeepSeek understands Chinese well, Gemini might give you surprises.

### Step 3: AI Organizes Output

After getting 4 responses, let AI quickly organize:

**Prompt**:
> I need an industry research report covering:
> 1. Industry overview (market size, growth rate)
> 2. Competition landscape (main players, market share)
> 3. Development trends (next 3-5 years)
> 4. Conclusions and suggestions
> Please organize in clear format, around 800 words.

### Step 4: Manual Optimization

AI is strong, but final human touch is needed:

**Checklist**:
- [ ] Data sources reasonable?
- [ ] Logic smooth?
- [ ] Format clear?
- [ ] Check for typos?
- [ ] Key data bolded?

## Advanced Tips

### Tip 1: Follow-up Questions

If AI answer isn't detailed enough:
> "About the XX data you mentioned, can you expand? Any specific cases?"

### Tip 2: Cross-verify

Different AIs might contradict:
> "ChatGPT says market size is 50B, DeepSeek says 80B—I need to verify the actual source."

### Tip 3: Structured Output

Let AI output in your required format:
> "Please list the top 5 competitors and their market shares in table format."
