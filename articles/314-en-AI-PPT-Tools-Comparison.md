## Advanced Tips

### 1. Better Results with Combined Use
My workflow:
- Gamma generates first draft
- Canva optimizes design
- PowerPoint fine-tunes details
- Export final version

### 2. More Specific Prompts Work Better
```
❌ Help me make a PPT
✅ Help me make a product launch PPT, topic is "Smartwatch Launch", including product introduction (5 pages), core features (health monitoring, message reminders, sports tracking, 5 pages), market analysis (3 pages), price and purchase method (2 pages), style is simple tech style
```

### 3. Generate Outline First Then PPT
Both Gamma and AiPPT support generating outline first, then generate complete PPT after confirming outline is satisfactory, avoiding rework.

### 4. Use AI Compare Plugin
Ask ChatGPT, Claude, DeepSeek, Kimi at once, let them provide PPT content and structure suggestions.

## FAQ

### Q1: Can AI-generated PPT be used directly?
A: Cannot be 100% used directly. AI generates a draft, need to:
- Check content accuracy
- Adjust data and charts
- Verify brand elements
- Beautify appropriately

### Q2: Is free version enough?
A:
- Gamma free version: 10 credits/month, enough
- Beautiful.ai: Features limited
- Xunfei Zhiwen: Completely free, enough
- Canva free version: Enough
- AiPPT: Limited

### Q3: What to note for commercial presentations?
A: Pay attention to copyright! Check template authorization. Commercial use best to use paid version or confirm templates are commercially usable.

### Q4: Which one do you recommend for beginners?
A: Gamma. Lowest barrier, fastest results, supports Chinese.

### Q5: Do these tools need VPN?
A:
- Gamma: Yes
- Beautiful.ai: Yes
- Xunfei Zhiwen: No
- Canva: No
- AiPPT: No
