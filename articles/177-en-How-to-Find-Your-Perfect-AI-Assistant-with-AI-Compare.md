## One Tab, Multiple AIs Compared

After installing AI Compare, you just need to:

1. Hit the shortcut key (Cmd+M on Mac, Ctrl+M on Windows)
2. Type in your question
3. Check the AI sites you want to compare
4. Click confirm

Then? Then you see ChatGPT, Claude, DeepSeek, Kimi, Doubao... all in one page.

Side by side. Clear as day.

No switching tabs. No retyping. No forgetting to ask one.

## Real Scenario: Writing a Job Application Email

Let me give you a personal example.

I was helping a friend review their resume, and they wanted to know how to write a more professional job application email. I used AI Compare to ask several AIs at once:

**Question:** "I'm a Java developer with 3 years of experience, applying for a backend position at ByteDance. Help me write a job application email, highlighting my distributed systems and microservices experience."

I checked ChatGPT, Claude, DeepSeek, and Doubao.

A few seconds later, all four answers appeared:

- ChatGPT had a standard structure, but felt a bit templated
- Claude sounded more natural, giving off "this person knows their stuff"
- DeepSeek had specific data, but the opening was a bit stiff
- Doubao's Chinese expressions were the most natural, but with fewer details

If I asked one by one, it might have taken 5-10 minutes. Now? 30 seconds done.

In the end, I combined the best parts from each AI to help my friend revise, and the result was better than any single AI's output alone.
