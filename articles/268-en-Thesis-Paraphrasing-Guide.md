## Why Single Model Isn't Enough?

Many people ask: Can't we just use one AI to rewrite?

The answer is: No.

Because single model output has "AI taste"—plagiarism check systems might detect it. More importantly, single model paraphrasing has monotonous sentence structures, making it easy for check systems to identify as machine-written.

The correct method is: **Cross paraphrasing with 4 AIs**.

Each AI has different expression styles. Using them alternately makes sentence expressions more diverse, making it harder for check systems to detect.

## Specific Operation Process

### Step 1: Identify Repetitive Paragraphs

Open the plagiarism check report and mark all red-marked paragraphs.

Process in paragraph order, don't copy everything to AI at once.

### Step 2: First AI Rewrite

Send the repetitive paragraph to ChatGPT with prompt:

> Please paraphrase the following content, change sentence structures, use different expressions while maintaining the original meaning:
> [Paste repetitive content]

### Step 3: Second AI Rewrite

Send the first rewrite result to another AI (e.g., Claude):

> Please further optimize this text to make expression more natural while maintaining academic nature:
> [Paste first rewrite content]

### Step 4: Manual Check

After AI finishes, must manually check:

- [ ] Core viewpoints preserved?
- [ ] Terminology accurate?
- [ ] Logic coherent?
- [ ] Any AI taste?

---

## Advanced Tips

### Tip 1: Process in Segments

Don't dump entire paragraphs to AI at once. Split into 2-3 sentence segments, rewrite separately, then combine.

### Tip 2: Combine with Deletion

If some sentence is really hard to rewrite, just delete it. Deleting non-essential sentences reduces similarity rate.

### Tip 3: Add Your Own Words

Paraphrasing isn't just rewriting—you can add your own analysis and perspectives. For example, add "The author believes..." in literature reviews.

### Tip 4: Convert to Tables/Images

If some high-similarity content is important, consider converting to tables or images. Current check systems can't recognize text in images.
