## Why Resume is Important?

Many people think they're capable but their resumes are too ordinary - HR simply can't see your highlights. AI can help solve this problem.

## Specific Methods for AI Resume Writing

### Method 1: Let AI Help Organize Your Experience

The biggest problem for many writing resumes isn't lack of experience, but not knowing how to express it.

**Steps**:
1. Organize your work and project experience into text
2. Tell AI your target position
3. Let AI help reorganize and express

**Prompt Example**:
```
I'm applying for product manager position, here's my work experience:
1. Responsible for requirements research and feature planning for company APP
2. Led 5-person team to complete 3 important projects
3. User retention rate increased by 20%

Please help me describe these experiences in more professional language, highlighting my abilities.
```

### Method 2: Let AI Help Optimize Wording

Same content, different expression, the effect is completely different.

**Before vs After Comparison**:

❌ Before:
```
Responsible for product-related work, did many features, user count improved.
```

✅ After:
```
Led core product feature planning and iteration. Through user research and data analysis, promoted 3 core features launch, helping increase DAU by 25%.
```

**Prompt Example**:
```
Please help optimize this resume description to be more professional and data-driven:
[paste your resume content]
Requirements: 1. Add specific data 2. Use professional terms 3. Highlight personal contributions
```

### Method 3: Let AI Help Match Job Requirements

The biggest fear when sending resumes is mismatched job requirements and your experience.

**Prompt Example**:
```
The job requirements I'm applying for:
1. 3+ years product experience
2. Project management capability
3. Data analysis capability
4. User growth experience

My experience is:
[paste your experience]

Please analyze my match level and point out which parts need strengthening in my resume.

## Common Resume Optimization Commands

### 1. Quantify Results
```
Change "improved user satisfaction" to more specific data description
```

### 2. Verb Upgrade
```
Change "responsible for", "did" to more powerful verbs like "led", "promoted", "achieved"
```

### 3. Structure Optimization
```
Help reorganize this experience in "background-action-result" structure
```

### 4. Keyword Matching
```
Help optimize this resume to match the following job requirements: [paste job requirements]
```

## Important Notes

### ❌ Don't Do This

1. **Don't let AI write entirely** - AI-written content is too generic, lacks personal characteristics
2. **Don't copy templates** - Templated resumes are easily recognized by HR
3. **Don't exaggerate facts** - You'll be exposed during interview questions
4. **Don't ignore formatting** - Good formatting makes HR comfortable reading

### ✅ Should Do This

1. **Write first yourself** - Organize main experiences and highlights
2. **Let AI optimize** - Optimize wording, highlight highlights, quantify data
3. **Review yourself** - Ensure content is true and logical
4. **Adjust for each application** - Don't send same resume everywhere

## Common Questions

### Q1: Will AI-written resumes be too generic?
A: Yes, so you must add your personal characteristics and real experiences on top of AI writing. AI is assistance, not replacement.

### Q2: How long should a resume be?
A: 1 page for fresh graduates, 2 pages for experienced. Don't be too long, HR doesn't have time.

### Q3: What's most important in a resume?
A: The most crucial is letting HR see your highlights in 6 seconds. Data expression and highlighting core achievements are most important.

### Q4: Can AI help with resume templates?
A: Yes, but recommend using professional resume template websites - AI mainly helps optimize content.
