## Mainstream AI Video Tools Comparison

### 1. Kling AI

**入口**: klingai.com

**Real thoughts**:
- From Kwai, the results are impressive
- Text-to-video is stable
- Image-to-video is strong
- Rich features with multiple generation options

**Pros**:
- Natural results
- Good motion range
- Decent free tier
- Best Chinese option

**Cons**:
- Wait times
- Variable speed

**Best for**:
- AI video beginners
- Content creators
- Short video professionals
