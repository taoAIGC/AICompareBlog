## Detailed Comparison

### 1. Runway Gen-3 - Industry Standard

Runway is a veteran player in the AI video space, evolving from Gen-1 to Gen-3,一步步 making it the industry standard.

**Pros**:
- Highest video quality currently, especially for人物动作和表情
- Rich control features (motion brush, camera control, etc.)
- Supports generating sound effects
- Enterprise-grade API, guaranteed stability
- Active community, many tutorials

**Cons**:
- Free tier is very limited (125 seconds/month)
- Generation speed is slow (waiting minutes is normal)
- Average Chinese prompt understanding
- Price is on the higher side ($15/month起)

**Who it's for**: Professional video creators, teams with budget, YouTubers needing cinematic effects

**My thoughts**: Runway Gen is really strong.-3's quality I used it to generate a "woman walking on the street" clip - skin texture, flowing hair, clothing wrinkles were all very natural, close to real footage.

But the problems are obvious: long wait times, too little free credit, average Chinese support.

---

### 2. Pika Labs - Speed Demon's Choice

Pika Labs is one of the hottest AI video products from last year, 主打的就是一个"快"字.

**Pros**:
- Fastest generation speed (seconds to output)
- Simple operation, user-friendly interface
- Supports image to video
- Rich voice synthesis features

**Cons**:
- Video length limit (max 3 seconds)
- Quality not as refined as Runway
- Complex scenes often fail

**Who it's for**: Short video creators, social media ops, users wanting quick output

**My thoughts**: Pika's speed is unbeatable, but quality depends on luck. Simple scenes are okay, complex ones often have problems.

---

### 3. Sora - The Bomb That's Coming

Sora is the heavy bomb OpenAI dropped this year,一经发布就震惊了整个AI圈.

**Pros**:
- Supports longest video (up to 60 seconds - longest currently)
- Very accurate understanding of the physical world
- Video quality comparable to cinematic level

**Cons**:
- Not yet open to the public
- Unknown when it will fully launch

**Who it's for**: Lucky ones waiting for access, AI enthusiasts following the frontier

**My thoughts**: Although I don't have Sora's beta access, I've seen many test videos and they're really impressive. Looking forward to its public release.

---

### 4. Luma Dream Machine - Rising Star

Luma is a new player that rose this year, and Dream Machine has gained a lot of attention since launch.

**Pros**:
- Relatively fast generation speed
- Good character consistency
- Acceptable free tier
- Supports image to video

**Cons**:
- Video quality not as good as Runway
- Sometimes has weird artificial traces
- Less community and tutorials

**Who it's for**: Creators needing fast video generation, users wanting free credits

**My thoughts**: Luma feels "usable but not amazing."

---

### 5. Sea Shell AI - Chinese Users' Blessing

Sea Shell AI is a product from ByteDance, specially optimized for Chinese users.

**Pros**:
- Accurate Chinese prompt understanding
- Stable domestic access (no VPN needed)
- Relatively affordable price

**Cons**:
- Video quality slightly lower than overseas products
- Video length limit (max 6 seconds)

**Who it's for**: Domestic creators, users who don't want hassle

**My thoughts**: Sea Shell AI's biggest advantage is being "接地气" - no need to flip walls, just type in Chinese and it works.
