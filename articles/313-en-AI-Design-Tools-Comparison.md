## 5款AI设计工具深度测评

### 1. Canva AI（最全能的設計助手）

**入口**：canva.com

**实测感受**：
- Enter "recruitment poster, recruiting programmers, blue technology style" and one will be generated in 10 seconds- The template library is huge and covers all scenarios.- Support Chinese! Support Chinese! Support Chinese!
**优点**：
- The most templates and the easiest to get started with-Support Chinese prompt words- The free version is enough- Especially convenient for making social media pictures
**缺点**：
- Complex designs still need to be adjusted manually- Domestic access is slightly slower
**适合场景**：
- Social media graphics- Simple poster- Resume, business card- Presentations
**价格**：免费版够用，Pro版12美元/月

---

### 2. Midjourney（AI绘图天花板）

**入口**：discord.com/invite/midjourney

**实测感受**：
- The quality of the generated pictures is really high- But you need to learn prompt words, and the threshold is higher than Canva- English prompt words work best
**优点**：
- Picture quality ceiling- Strong artistic sense- Many community references
**缺点**：
- Requires scientific Internet access- English prompt words- Steep learning curve- You cannot make a poster template directly
**适合场景**：
- Creative picture generation- Concept design- artistic illustrations
**价格**：10美元/月起

---

### 3. Adobe Firefly（Adobe官方AI）

**入口**：firefly.adobe.com

**实测感受**：
- After all, he is the son of Adobe, seamlessly connected with PS and AI- The "Generate Fill" function is too powerful- But the function is still being improved
**优点**：
- Perfect integration with Adobe Family Bucket- Commercial security (Adobe copyright commitment)- Partial redraw function is easy to use
**缺点**：
- Not as rich in features as Midjourney- Adobe account required- Domestic access is unstable
**适合场景**：
- Designer assistance- Picture modification- Commercial design projects
**价格**：免费体验中，正式版待发布

---

### 4. Microsoft Designer（微软出品）

**入口**：designer.microsoft.com

**实测感受**：
- Totally free! What kind of bike do you want?- Integrate with Copilot for intelligent recommendations- Template quality is getting higher and higher
**优点**：
- completely free- You can use it with a Microsoft account- Template quality is good- Support Chinese
**缺点**：
- Started late, template library is not as good as Canva- Sometimes generation is slow
**适合场景**：
- Daily office pictures- social media- Simple poster
**价格**：免费

---

### 5. Remove.bg（专注抠图）

**入口**：remove.bg

**实测感受**：
- A tool for cutting out pictures, who knows who uses it- Complete character cutout in 5 seconds- Support batch processing
**优点**：
- Professional cutout effect- fast- Support API
**缺点**：
- Single function- Free version has limitations
**适合场景**：
- Product picture cutout- Change the background of your ID photo- E-commerce main image
**价格**：免费版每月50张，付费每月9美元起

## 常见问题

### Q1：AI会取代设计师吗？
A: No. AI is a tool that helps designers improve efficiency, but it is not a replacement. Creativity, aesthetics, and communication still depend on people.
### Q2：免费版够用吗？
A:- Canva free version: enough- Midjourney: Fee required- Microsoft Designer: free and sufficient- Remove.bg: with restrictions
### Q3：做商用设计要注意什么？
A: Pay attention to copyright! The commercial use of Midjourney is controversial, but Adobe Firefly promises safe commercial use.
### Q4：新手推荐从哪个开始？
A: Canva AI. The threshold is the lowest and the effects can be seen the fastest.
### Q5：这些工具需要翻墙吗？
A:- Canva: Not required- Midjourney: required- Adobe Firefly: required- Microsoft Designer: Not required- Remove.bg: Not required
