## The Catch

AI programming tools are powerful, but they can't replace you.

They can write code, but they can't design architecture;
They can fix bugs, but they can't understand the business;
They can improve efficiency, but they can't help you grow.

My habit is: AI writes basic code → I do architecture design and optimization → AI helps me write tests.

After all, you're the one writing code. AI is just your copilot.
