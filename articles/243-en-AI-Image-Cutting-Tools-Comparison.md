## 详细对比

### 1. remove.bg——抠图界的"老大哥"

remove.bg is the pioneer in the field of AI cutout. It is the most famous and its effects have been imitated.

**优点**：
- The cutout effect is the most stable, especially for product images
- Supports various types such as portraits, objects, products, etc.
- Complete API interface, suitable for batch processing
- Batch processing function (paid version)
- No need for scientific Internet access (accessible within the country)
- Supports two modes: transparent background and solid color background
- Plug-in support (Photoshop, Figma, etc.)

**缺点**：
- The free version can only download small size images
- High-definition pictures require payment (starting at $0.9/picture)
- Portrait hair processing is occasionally not delicate enough
- Does not support adjustment directly in the editor
- Additional charges apply for batch processing

**适合谁**：电商卖家、设计师、需要批量抠图的用户

**我的使用感受**：remove.bg是"稳重型"选手。

I have used remove.bg to process hundreds of e-commerce main images, and I can sum it up in one word: stable. Whether it’s electronic products, clothing, shoes, bags, or food and beverages, the results are pretty reliable.

The most commonly used scenario is e-commerce main image processing. Upload the product image, wait a second or two, and the background will be completely removed. The edges are also handled very naturally, without the feeling of being "cut by a knife".

It supports batch uploading, up to 20 photos at a time. This function is very worry-free when dealing with cross-border e-commerce product distribution on multiple platforms.

The disadvantage is that high-definition pictures cost money. The downloaded image in the free version is only about 626x626 pixels, which is barely enough for sending main images to Amazon and Taobao, but not enough for fine retouching.

---

### 2. Clipdrop——功能最全的"全能选手"

Clipdrop is an AI photo retouching tool that will emerge in 2023. It has a full range of functions, and cutout is only one of its functions.

**优点**：
- Not only can you cut out pictures, but you can also use AI to enlarge pictures and change the background.
- High image quality with good detail retention
-Supports relighting effect
- Batch processing function
- Both stable version and API available
- The free version is relatively generous
- No need to surf the Internet

**缺点**：
- More expensive than remove.bg
- The interface is in English and is not very friendly to domestic users.
- Some advanced features require Pro version
- Domestic access speed is occasionally slow

**适合谁**：专业设计师、需要多种AI修图功能的用户

**我的使用感受**：Clipdrop是"全能型"选手。

What attracts me most about Clipdrop is its supporting functions. In addition to cutting out the image, you can also use AI to expand the image (extend the image), change the background (to any scene), and re-light. The combination of these functions can create many effects that previously took PS half a day to complete.

For example, I once made a product display picture for a customer, and the background of the original picture was too cluttered. After cutting out the image with Clipdrop, I directly changed it to a solid color background and then adjusted the light. It took no more than 5 minutes to complete the image that used to take half a day to edit.

The free version has a monthly processing limit of 100 times, which is enough for individual users’ daily use. The paid version starts at $9/month, which is a good value for money.

---

### 3. Photoroom——电商专用的"神器"

Photoroom is an image processing tool specially built for e-commerce. Cutting out images is just a basic function.

**优点**：
- Specifically optimized for e-commerce scenarios
- Built-in massive e-commerce templates
- Generate main image + details page with one click
- Support batch processing
- Good mobile APP experience
- Powerful background replacement function
- No need to surf the Internet

**缺点**：
- The cutout effect is not as precise as remove.bg
- Free version has watermark
- Premium templates require payment
- Mainly oriented to e-commerce scenarios, with general versatility

**适合谁**：电商卖家、亚马逊/淘宝/拼多多店主

**我的使用感受**：Photoroom是"电商专属"选手。

The best thing about Photoroom is its one-stop e-commerce solution. Not only can you cut out images, but you can also directly apply templates, add text, and borders to generate main images that meet the specifications of each platform.

For example, Amazon's main image requires a white background and a pixel size of 1000x1000 or more, which can be used directly after processing with Photoroom. Taobao’s main image requires 750x1000, which can also be done with one click.

It also supports batch generation of main images. Before launching a new product, I could process 50 pictures at once. Using Photoroom’s batch function, I could finish them all in half an hour, which was very efficient.

The disadvantage is that the cutout accuracy is slightly worse. Some products have complicated edges and may have slight flaws when cut out, requiring manual adjustment.

---

### 4. Adobe Remove Background——Adobe生态的"亲儿子"

Adobe Remove Background is an online cutout tool officially launched by Adobe and integrated into Adobe Express.

**优点**：
- Adobe brand endorsement, quality guaranteed
- Integrated Adobe Express design capabilities
- Support PSD transparent background export
- No need to install software, just use the web version directly
- Adobe account can log in directly
- Conveniently combined with other Adobe tools

**缺点**：
- Adobe account required
- The cutout effect is not as good as remove.bg
- Relatively simple functions
- Domestic access speed is average
- The free version has limited quota

**适合谁**：Adobe全家桶用户、设计师

**我的使用感受**：Adobe Remove Background是"生态型"选手。

If you use Photoshop or Illustrator, Adobe Remove Background is really convenient. After cutting out the image, drag it directly to PS to continue editing, without having to drag it back and forth in the middle.

The cutout effect... to be honest is not as good as remove.bg. Especially when dealing with details such as portrait hair and product edges, Adobe's performance is a bit awkward.

But the accompanying Adobe Express is quite nice. You can directly create posters, puzzles, and social media images on it, and cut out images and design in one stop.

---

### 5. 稿定设计——国内用户的"贴心选择"

Draft Design is a domestic design platform, and AI cutout is one of its functions.

**优点**：
- Domestic access is fast
- Support Chinese interface
- Rich supporting design templates
- Membership prices are relatively cheap
- Customer service responds quickly
- Many materials available for commercial use

**缺点**：
- The cutout effect is not as good as foreign professional tools
- Hair and other details are average
- Advanced features require membership
- Template quality varies

**适合谁**：国内电商卖家、设计师、新手用户

**我的使用感受**：稿定设计是"接地气"选手。

The final design is particularly friendly to domestic users. The interface is in Chinese and the operation logic is in line with domestic habits. Unlike those foreign tools, you can’t figure out where to look for functions after looking at them for a long time.

I often use it for Taobao main images and detail pages. There is a rich template library, many of which are free. As a gift, the cutout function works pretty well.

The disadvantage is that the accuracy is slightly worse. Once when I was processing a group of portrait photos, the hair edges were not clean enough, so I ended up redoing them using remove.bg.

---

### 6. Canva——设计小白的"救星"

Canva is an online design platform, and the cutout function was recently added.

**优点**：
- Beautiful interface and simple operation
- Supporting design with powerful functions
- Massive template materials
- Convenient for team collaboration
- The free version is enough
- No need to surf the Internet

**缺点**：
- The cutout effect is average
- Functions are scattered and not focused enough
- Domestic access speed is unstable
- Advanced features are expensive

**适合谁**：设计小白、社交媒体运营、非专业设计师

**我的使用感受**：Canva是"入门级"选手。

Canva's cutout function is suitable for those "hands-on" needs. For example, make a poster and cut out a picture. No need to specifically open another tool.

But if you are looking for cutting-out accuracy, Canva is not good enough. It can handle simple product images just fine, but it can’t handle complex scenes.

Canva's biggest advantage is its powerful design. After cutting out the pictures, you can directly create posters, illustrations, and social media content, one-stop service.

## 我的推荐

- **Professional e-commerce cutout**: remove.bg
- **Multi-functional AI photo editing**: Clipdrop
- **One-stop e-commerce main image**: Photoroom
- **Adobe eco-users**: Adobe Remove Background
- **Domestic e-commerce/newbie**: finalize the design
- **Design by the way**: Canva

### 按预算推荐

- **Budget 0**: Canva + Final Design Free Edition
- **Budget $9/mo**: Clipdrop or Photoroom
- **Mainly cut out pictures**: remove.bg pays per picture

### 按用户群体推荐

- **E-commerce seller**: remove.bg + Photoroom combination
- **Designer**: Clipdrop + Photoshop
- **Newbie**: Canva or finalize the design
- **Cross-border e-commerce**: remove.bg + Clipdrop combination

## 总结

AI cutout tools are now very mature. There is no perfect tool, only the best choice for current needs.

- **If you want stable effect**: select remove.bg
- **Want more functions**: Choose Clipdrop
- **Want one-stop e-commerce**: Choose Photoroom
- **Want domestic fast**: Select the draft and finalize the design
- **Want to do design by the way**: Choose Canva

My suggestion is: use remove.bg for professional cutouts, e-commerce sellers use Photoroom for the main image, and domestic users can use the draft design to save worry. Several tools are used together for the best results.
