## 模板1：角色扮演法

Let AI act as a professional, and the output quality will immediately improve.

❌ Incorrect usage:
```
Help me write a product copy
```

✅ Correct usage:
```
You are a top copywriter with 10 years of experience, specializing in e-commerce product copywriting. Please write a selling point copy for our smart watch. Requirements:
1. Highlight 3 core selling points
2. Use life-like scene descriptions
3. End with a call to action
4. Word count within 300 words
```

**Why does it work? **

When you give an AI a professional identity, it will automatically invoke that character's thinking patterns and expression habits.

Let AI play the role of marketing director, product manager, or industry expert, and the professionalism of the output content will be completely different.

---

## 模板2：子弹要点法

Let the AI ​​list the main points first, and then expand into the complete content.

❌ Incorrect usage:
```
Write an article about time management
```

✅ Correct usage:
```
Step 1: Please list 10 core skills of time management (no more than 5 words each)
Step 2: Please write a 500-word detailed explanation of the third technique "Pomodoro Technique", including: principle introduction, operation steps, applicable scenarios, and common misunderstandings
```

**Why does it work? **

Making an outline first and then filling in the content is a basic skill for professional writers.

The same logic applies to AI: first you need the skeleton, and then you fill it with flesh and blood.

And you can choose the part you want to delve into most based on the key points listed by AI and continue to expand.

---

## 模板3：对比分析法

Let AI analyze from multiple angles and provide more comprehensive content.

❌ Incorrect usage:
```
Analyze this product
```

✅ Correct usage:
```
Please analyze our new product "Smart Water Cup" from the following 5 dimensions:
1. Pain points of target users
2. Core competitive advantages
3. Pricing strategy suggestions
4. Possible market risks
5. Promotion strategy suggestions

Use 200 words for each dimension to analyze, and finally give a comprehensive score (out of 10 points)
```

**Why does it work? **

A single perspective is easily biased, and multi-dimensional analysis is more comprehensive.

Moreover, this structured output method allows you to grasp the key points at a glance, saving a lot of organizing time.

---

## 模板4：案例嵌入式

Let AI add real cases to the content to increase persuasiveness.

❌ Incorrect usage:
```
Write an inspirational story about persistence
```

✅ Correct usage:
```
Write an inspirational story about "persistence", requiring:
1. The protagonist is an ordinary person (not a well-known person like Jack Ma and Ma Huateng)
2. Success after 3 major failures
3. The comparison between failure and success should be specific (data such as income, status, etc.)
4. There should be an inspiring conclusion at the end.
5. Word count: 800 words
```

**Why does it work? **

Real cases are more convincing than empty preaching.

When you explicitly ask for specific details, the AI ​​will create a more believable story rather than generalities.

---

## 模板5：迭代优化法

Don’t think about getting it right in one step, it’s more efficient to iterate step by step.

❌ Incorrect usage:
```
Help me write the perfect brand story
```

✅ Correct usage:
```
Step 1: Please write a brand story (100 words) for our coffee brand in a warm and touching style
Step 2: This story is good, but I don’t think it’s impactful enough. Please add some conflict elements (such as difficulties in starting a business)
Step 3: Very good, but the ending is too long. Please shorten it to less than 20 words.

Please keep the story intact in the final version
```

**Why does it work? **

Good articles are all made by editing, and AI writing is no exception.

Give AI specific feedback with each iteration (what is good and what needs to be changed), and it will understand your style better and better.

## 常见问题

**Q: Will the prompt word be too long to confuse the AI? **

A: No.

AI is very capable of processing long texts. What it is afraid of is unclear information, not too much information.

The key is to break down the requirements clearly and don’t pile them all on top of each other.

**Q: Do you have to write such complicated reminder words every time? **

A: No need.

Simple requirements, simple questions:
```
Help me write a Slogan, keywords: young, trendy
```

Only use the complete template for complex requirements:
```
You are a senior brand strategy expert. Please design a complete brand story for our new brand...
```

**Q: What do you think about the content written by AI? **

A: Two suggestions:

1. Give more specific constraints (style, length, audience)
2. Use iterative method to optimize in multiple rounds

**Q: Do different AIs use the same prompt words to have the same effect? **

A: There will be differences.

ChatGPT is more balanced, Claude is better at logical analysis, and DeepSeek performs well in the Chinese context.

## 进阶技巧

### 技巧1：给出"反面教材"

Telling the AI ​​what you don't want is equally important.

```
Please write a product review. It should be an objective and fair analysis of the advantages and disadvantages. Avoid the feeling of writing a soft article, and do not over-stuff adjectives.
```

### 技巧2：指定参考对象

Let the AI ​​learn a specific style.

```
Please follow the style of "Little Red Book Blogger" @老高 and write a note about the realization of side business
```

### 技巧3：让AI自己检查

Let the AI ​​review itself after output.

```
Please write your answer first, then check for typos, grammatical issues, and evaluate whether the content meets my requirements.
```
