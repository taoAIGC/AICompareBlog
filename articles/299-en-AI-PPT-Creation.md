## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Define the PPT Topic and Content

Before using AI, clarify the basic content:

**Things to figure out:**
- What is this PPT about? (topic)
- Who is it for? (audience)
- What goal do you want to achieve? (persuade? introduce? report?)
- What are the main content points?

**Tip**: You can first create a simple outline on paper or in a document — doesn't need to be detailed, just have a structure.

### Step 2: Choose the Right Tool

Select based on your scenario:

- **Business proposals, reports to leadership**: Gamma is recommended — professional templates
- **Creative displays, personal branding**: Tome is recommended — unique style
- **Daily office work, simple reports**: Meitu AI PPT or Canva AI
- **Need to integrate with existing Office documents**: iSlide AI is recommended

### Step 3: Give Clear Instructions

This is the most critical step! Only with the right instructions can AI understand what you need.

**Universal formula:**
```
Help me create a PPT about [topic], for [audience], mainly for [scenario], with a [formal/lively/professional] style, including: [main sections]
```

**Specific examples:**

**Scenario 1: Work Report**
```
Help me create a quarterly work summary PPT, for department leaders and colleagues, for monthly meeting reporting. The style should be professional and concise, including: 1. This quarter's work completion 2. Challenges faced and solutions 3. Next quarter's work plan. Use data to support points.
```

**Scenario 2: Project Proposal**
```
Help me create a new product launch promotion plan PPT, for the marketing and operations team, for project kickoff meeting. The style should be creative and lively, including: 1. Market background 2. Target user profile 3. Promotion strategy 4. Budget and timeline 5. Expected results.
```

**Scenario 3: Training Materials**
```
Help me create a new employee onboarding training PPT, for company new hires, for onboarding training sessions. The style should be friendly and approachable, including: 1. Company introduction 2. Corporate culture 3. Company policies 4. Department responsibilities 5. FAQ.
```

### Step 4: Review and Adjust

After AI generates the PPT, be sure to check these areas:

1. **Content accuracy**: Confirm the AI-written content matches your actual situation
2. **Logical flow**: Read through from start to finish — is the logic coherent?
3. **Image matching**: Check if images are appropriate; replace any that aren't suitable
4. **Brand consistency**: If there's company branding (VI), check if colors and fonts match
5. **Typos**: AI can also make mistakes — don't forget to check

## Common PPT Structure Templates

### Work Report PPT
```
1. Cover (title + presenter + date)
2. Work overview (summarize in one sentence)
3. Key achievements (1-3 highlights, preferably with data)
4. Detailed work content (bullet points)
5. Problems encountered and solutions (demonstrate problem-solving ability)
6. Next period plan (next work priorities)
7. Closing page (thanks + Q&A session)

### Project Proposal PPT
```
1. Cover (project name + team)
2. Background and pain points (why do this)

4. Market analysis (how big is the opportunity)
5. Execution plan (how to do it)
6. Resource requirements (what support is needed)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: What if AI-generated PPTs all look the same?**
A: You can adjust details after generation, such as changing images, colors, or layout. You can also try multiple tools to find the one that best matches your style.

**Q2: What if AI-written text is too formal/informal?**
A: You can specify tone requirements in your instructions, such as "use plain, easy-to-understand language" or "use formal business tone." You can also edit it yourself after generation.

**Q3: Can I replace images I don't like?**
A: Absolutely! AI-generated images are just references — you can replace them with your own images. Most tools support image replacement.

**Q4: What about privacy and security concerns?**
A: You can choose enterprise-level tools with privacy policies, or avoid putting highly sensitive information in your PPTs. General work report-level content is usually fine.

Some AI PPT tools also support generating speech drafts/notes, helping you organize what to say. Particularly useful during rehearsals.

If you need to create a series of PPTs on similar topics, you can first generate one you're satisfied with, then modify based on it — much more efficient.
