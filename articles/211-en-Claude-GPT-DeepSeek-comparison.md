## Real Comparison: Three Major AIs

### 1. Claude — The Most Human-Like AI

**My experience**:
Claude is my daily driver. The biggest takeaway? It really gets me.

**What it's good at**:
- Long text analysis: Throw a research paper at it, and it'll explain everything clearly
- Writing style adaptation: Ask it to write in a specific style, and it's almost indistinguishable from human writing
- Code understanding: Explains complex code logic exceptionally well

**Usage feelings**:
- Natural conversation style, doesn't feel like talking to a machine
- Proactively considers your潜在 needs
- Strong context understanding, handles long conversations without losing track

**Drawbacks**:
- Slightly weaker Chinese expression than GPT
- Limited free tier
- Sometimes too conservative, hesitates to make conclusions

**Best for**: Users needing deep analysis, long text processing, professional writing

**My rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

### 2. ChatGPT — The All-Round Veteran

**GPT is the "veteran" of the AI world—most well-rounded, but sometimes feels too "standardized."**

**What it's good at**:
- General Q&A: Answers everything, very stable
- Creative writing: Great imagination, excellent language skills
- Code generation: High quality, fewer bugs
- Translation: Top-tier language capabilities

**Usage feelings**:
- Fast response times, rarely fails
- Wide knowledge base, can discuss any topic
- Mature ecosystem, plugins and APIs work great

**Drawbacks**:
- Sometimes too "corporate" in tone, lacks personality
- Chinese responses feel "translated"
- Paid version is pricey

**Best for**: Users needing versatile tools, stability, willing to pay

**My rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

### 3. DeepSeek — The Best Value

**My experience**:
DeepSeek is a pleasant surprise from China. Free, powerful—it's earned my respect.

**What it's good at**:
- Chinese conversation: Natural expressions, not stiff
- Programming assistance: Good code quality, explains thinking
- Quick queries: Fast responses, great for simple questions
- Saving money: Free! What more do you want?

**Usage feelings**:
- Excellent Chinese understanding
- Quick responses
- Completely free, low barrier to entry

**Drawbacks**:
- Not as strong with long texts
- Occasionally struggles with complex reasoning
- Ecosystem less developed than the other two

**Best for**: Budget-conscious users, Chinese-language users, simple needs

**My rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
