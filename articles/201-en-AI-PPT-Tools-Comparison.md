## 5 Popular AI PPT Tools Tested

I spent over $70 subscribing to these tools. Here's the real experience:

### 1. Gamma — For Design Lovers

**Experience**:
- Generated PPTs have high visual appeal and professional design
- Supports Chinese, usable globally
- Many theme templates to choose from

**Pros**:
- Beautiful interface, easy to operate
- Rich export formats (PDF, PPT, images)
- Supports online presentations

**Cons**:
- Free version has limits
- Occasionally has Chinese grammar issues
- Needs VPN in China

**Best for**: Client presentations, proposal demos

**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

### 2. Beautiful.ai — For Professionals

**Experience**:
- Very professional template designs
- Powerful auto-layout features
- Great for business scenarios

**Pros**:

- Good team collaboration features

**Cons**:
- Learning curve needed
- Average Chinese support
- Pricey

**Best for**: Corporate reports, business proposals, market analysis

**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Tips to Avoid Pitfalls

1. **Don't fully rely on AI**: AI-generated content needs human review, especially data and terminology
2. **Watch for copyright**: Some templates have commercial restrictions, verify before corporate use
3. **Save source files**: Online AI tools may be unstable, export backups regularly
