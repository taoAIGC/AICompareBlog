## Introduction

Everyone in new media operations knows content creation is the most time-consuming work. Revising articles over and over, running out of ideas is the norm. Today I'll talk about how to use AI to help us create content efficiently and free our hands.

---

## What AI Creation Can Do

### 1. Write First Drafts
When you don't know where to start, let AI write a first draft first, then you revise and improve - much faster than starting from scratch.

### 2. Rewrite and Optimize
Finished writing but think it's not good enough? Throw it to AI and let it optimize the expression - more efficient than revising repeatedly.

### 3. Brainstorming
Don't know what topics to write about? Let AI give you ideas - 10 or 8 topics, there's always something that can inspire you.

### 4. Title Creation
A good title can make or break an article. AI can help you generate multiple title options.

### 5. Ending Enhancement
Can't write good endings? AI can help summarize and enhance, giving your article more depth.

## AI Creation Tips

### Tip 1: Set AI's Role

**Wrong**:
"Help me write an article"

**Correct**:
"You are a senior new media editor with 10 years of experience, expert in writing practical articles, help me write an article about..."

### Tip 2: Provide Enough Background

**Wrong**:
"Write a piece of copy"

**Correct**:
"We're a workplace education public account, target users are 25-35 year olds, we're doing resume tips topics recently, help me write an article about..."

### Tip 3: Process in Steps

**Wrong**:
"Help me write a complete WeChat article"

**Correct**:
1. First let AI suggest 3 topics
2. After choosing, let AI write an outline
3. After outline confirmed, let AI write first draft
4. After first draft done, let AI optimize

### Tip 4: Specify Output Format

**Wrong**:
"Summarize this"

**Correct**:
"Summarize the pros and cons of the following content in table format"

## Important Notes

### ❌ Don't Do This

1. **Don't Copy Directly** - Always revise what AI writes, combine with your own thoughts
2. **Don't Ignore Facts** - AI can fabricate data, always verify
3. **Don't Ignore Platform Characteristics** - Each platform has different styles, adjust accordingly
4. **Don't Over-Rely** - AI is assistance, not replacement for your thinking

### ✅ Should Do This

1. **Think First Yourself** - Core ideas should come from you, AI helps express
2. **Verify Carefully** - Verify data and cases
3. **Add Personal Touches** - Add your experiences and style for recognition
4. **Keep Learning** - Study excellent content, improve your taste and judgment

## Common Questions

### Q1: Will AI-written content be too formulaic?
A: Yes, so you must add your own perspectives, cases, and style on top of AI's writing for recognition.

### Q2: Will AI fabricate data?
A: It might - always verify. AI sometimes fabricates data and quotes, don't believe everything.

### Q3: Is AI-created content considered original?
A: Yes, AI is just a tool. The core ideas and content framework are provided by you, which means AI helps improve efficiency.

### Q4: Can AI completely replace humans?
A: No. AI can help write first drafts and optimize expression, but core topics, ideas, and cases still need to come from you.
