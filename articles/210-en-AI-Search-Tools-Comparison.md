## 5 Popular AI Search Tools Tested

I personally tested these tools. Here's my real experience:

### 1. Perplexity AI — The AI Search Pioneer

**My experience**:
- Called the "ceiling of AI search"
- Accurate answers with clear sources
- Great for academic research and professional queries

**Pros**:
- Answers include source links for verification
- Supports follow-up questions and deep research
- Clean interface, no ads

**Cons**:
- Free tier has usage limits
- Chinese search experience is average
- Sometimes confidently gives wrong answers

**Best for**: Academic researchers, professionals, in-depth queries

**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

### 2. ChatGPT Search — The Old Player's New Battle

**My experience**:
- OpenAI's finally entered the search game
- Inherits ChatGPT's natural conversation ability
- Much more practical after getting real-time web access

**Pros**:
- Smooth conversation experience
- Can ask follow-up questions
- Accurate real-time information

**Cons**:
- Just launched, features still improving
- Unstable access in China
- Answers sometimes too lengthy

**Best for**: ChatGPT users, daily queries, writing assistance

**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

### 3. Metaso AI Search — China's Rising Star

**My experience**:
- A solid AI search from China
- Great understanding of Chinese
- Perfect for domestic users

**Pros**:
- Excellent Chinese search experience
- Works without VPN
- Good accuracy

**Cons**:
- Compared to foreign products, source coverage is limited
- Advanced features require payment
- Still building ecosystem

**Best for**: Chinese users, students, Chinese-language researchers

**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

### 4. TianGong AI — The All-Rounder

**My experience**:
- AI product from Kunlun Tech
- Integrated search + conversation + creation
- Comprehensive features

**Pros**:
- Complete product ecosystem
- Multiple search modes
- Stable domestic access

**Cons**:
- Inconsistent answer quality
- Sometimes hallucinates
- Interface feels complex

**Best for**: Users needing comprehensive AI capabilities, domestic users

**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐

---

### 5. 360 AI Search — The Veteran Strikes Back

**My experience**:
- 360's AI search product
- Leverages their search expertise
- Good as a backup option

**Pros**:
- Stable domestic access
- Based on 360's search data
- Free to use

**Cons**:
- Weaker AI capabilities compared to others
- Still has ads
- Less intelligent than dedicated AI search

**Best for**: Users needing a backup search tool

**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐

## My Usage Suggestions

### Choose by Scenario

| Scenario | Recommended Tool | Reason |
|----------|------------------|--------|
| Academic research | Perplexity AI | Accurate, clear sources |
| Daily conversation | ChatGPT Search | Smooth experience, follow-up support |
| Chinese queries | Metaso AI Search | Great Chinese, no VPN needed |
| General needs | TianGong AI | Comprehensive features |
| Backup option | 360 AI Search | Stable and free |

### Tips to Avoid Pitfalls

1. **AI search isn't perfect**: Always verify important information
2. **Check sources**: Good answers include sources, be wary of those without
3. **Compare multiple tools**: Search the same question with different tools and pick the best
4. **Combine with traditional search**: When AI fails, traditional engines still work
