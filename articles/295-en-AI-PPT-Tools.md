## Practical Demo

### Scenario: Quarterly Work Report

**Step 1: Determine Content**
Assume you need a quarterly work report, first think about what to include:
- Work completed this quarter
- Achievements made
- Existing problems
- Next quarter plans

**Step 2: Choose Tool and Enter Instructions**

Using Kimi PPT as example:
```
Help me create a quarterly work report PPT including:
1. Main projects completed this quarter (3)
2. Performance data (need 3 specific numbers)
3. Existing problems and solutions
4. Next quarter work plan

Requirements: Business style, concise and professional
```

**Step 3: Check and Modify**

After AI generates, must check:
1. **Content completeness** - Any missing points?
2. **Data accuracy** - AI may fabricate data, must verify
3. **Logic flow** - Is the order reasonable?
4. **Image appropriateness** - Can replace if not suitable

**Step 4: Fine-tune**

- Adjust font sizes
- Replace unsuitable images
- Add details AI missed
- Add company logo

## Important Notes

### ❌ Don't Do This

1. **Don't completely rely on AI** - Always check and verify AI-generated content
2. **Don't ignore data accuracy** - AI may fabricate data and quotes, always verify
3. **Don't ignore company style** - Each company has its own VI, AI doesn't know your standards
4. **Don't copy templates directly** - Templates are just references, add your own content

### ✅ Should Do This

1. **Create your own outline first** - Think of core content yourself, let AI help fill in
2. **Provide detailed background** - More background = more accurate output
3. **Carefully verify content** - Facts and data must be verified by you
4. **Add personal style** - Add your own cases and opinions for more persuasion

### Q1: Will AI-generated PPTs all look the same?
A: Yes, so try different tools and choose one that suits your style. You can also manually adjust.

A: No, AI only creates drafts. Must check and modify, add your own content before using.
