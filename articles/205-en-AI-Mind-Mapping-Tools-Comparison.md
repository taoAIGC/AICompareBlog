## Real Test of 5 Mainstream AI Mind Mapping Tools

I personally tested these tools, here's my real experience:

### 1. MindMeister — The Veteran Player

**User Experience**:
- Veteran in the mind mapping field
- Full-featured, familiar interface
- AI features were added later

**Pros**:
- Stable and reliable
- Rich templates
- Strong collaboration features

**Cons**:
- AI features are weak
- Free quota is limited
- Chinese interface is average

**Best For**: Team collaboration, traditional mind mapping, daily office work

**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

### 2. Miro AI — The Collaboration King

**User Experience**:
- Leader in online whiteboard field
- AI features integrated into whiteboard
- Great for team brainstorming sessions

**Pros**:
- Top-notch collaboration experience
- Huge template library
- Strong integration capabilities

**Cons**:
- Requires registration
- Speed depends on network
- Mind mapping is just one small feature

**Best For**: Team collaboration, brainstorming, project management

**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
