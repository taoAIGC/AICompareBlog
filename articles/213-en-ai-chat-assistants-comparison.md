## Real comparison: Five major AI conversation assistants

### 1. Claude — the deepest thinking AI

**Claude is now my daily go-to. The biggest feeling? It is really "thinking". **

**Area of Expertise**:
- In-depth analysis: If you ask a question, it will give you a clear analysis
- Logical reasoning: strong ability to handle complex problems
- Code understanding: technical issues explained clearly
- Long article writing: Writing long articles is especially reliable
- Document processing: PDF and screenshots can be read directly

**Using experience**:
- Top-notch thinking depth, not the kind of AI that only gives answers
- Strong ability to maintain context, long conversations are not easy to go off track
- It feels like a "reliable expert" is helping you
- The latest Claude 3.5 Sonnet is particularly powerful

**shortcoming**:
- The free version quota is really pitiful
- Domestic access is not stable (sometimes you have to circumvent the firewall)
-Sometimes you are too conservative and dare not draw conclusions

**Suitable scenarios**: in-depth analysis, complex issues, professional writing, technical consulting

**My Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

### 2. ChatGPT — the most ecologically powerful AI

**GPT is the "veteran" in the AI world - it can do everything, but it just doesn't go deep enough. **

**Area of Expertise**:
- Quick Q&A: Check information, ask questions, and get answers quickly
- Translation: Good translation quality in various languages
- Learning aids: explain concepts and teach you new things
- Creative writing: I am good at writing novels and making up stories.
- Plug-in ecology: There are many plug-ins and strong scalability

**Using experience**:
- Fast response and few rollovers
- Dare to answer questions in any field, don’t pick jobs
- Mature ecosystem and many plug-ins (although many require payment)
- GPT-4o has strong comprehensive capabilities

**shortcoming**:
- In-depth analysis is average, and the answers given tend to be superficial.
- Sometimes it’s hard to understand Chinese
- The paid version is getting more and more expensive
- Free version often has restricted usage

**Suitable scenarios**: Quickly look up information, learn new technologies, translation, creative writing

**My Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### 3. DeepSeek — your first choice for saving money

**DeepSeek is a surprise for domestic AI. It’s free + you can take a taxi, so what kind of bike do you need? **

**Area of Expertise**:
- Chinese dialogue: Ask questions in Chinese and the replies will be very natural
- Code generation: Very good at writing code
- Simple Q&A: Handle daily problems well
- Save money: Free! This is so fragrant

**Using experience**:
- Ridiculously strong Chinese understanding ability
- Fast response
- Completely free, very low threshold
-Both the web client and API are easy to use

**shortcoming**:
- The ability to analyze complex problems is weaker than Claude
- Sometimes "seriously talking nonsense"
- Long conversations can easily go astray
- Ecology is still under construction

**Suitable scenarios**: simple questions and answers, daily use, student parties, limited budget

**My Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
