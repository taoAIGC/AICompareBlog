## Ranking

### 🥇 #1: Kimi - King of Long Text Processing

**One-line review**: Document reading god, 2 million character context for free

**Pros**:
- Free version supports 2 million character context, others charge for this
- Can upload 50 files at once
- Fast response, strong understanding
- Clean interface, simple to use

**Cons**:
- Web search not as good as Baidu
- Voice input experience is average

**Best for**: People who need to read lots of documents, students, researchers

**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
