## Step-by-Step Method

### Step 1: Organize This Week's/Month's Work Content

Before writing reports, first list what you did this week/month:

**How to organize**:
- Which projects you completed
- Which meetings you participated in
- Problems encountered (optional)
- Plans for next week/month

**Tip**: It's best to take brief notes daily, otherwise you won't remember specific work when writing reports.

### Step 2: Choose the Right AI Tool

| Tool | Features | Best For |
|------|----------|----------|
| Kimi | Strong long text processing | Detailed reports with lots of content |
| Tongyi Qianwen | Stable and reliable | Regular work reports |
| Yiyan | Web search for data | Reports needing data supplementation |
| iFlytek Spark | Voice input | People who prefer voice |

### Step 3: Give Clear Instructions

This is the most critical step! Better instructions = better AI output.

**Universal formula**:
```
You are a [profession], help me write a [weekly/monthly report], including: [specific content], requirements: [format/length/tone]
```

**Specific examples**:

**Scenario 1: Writing Weekly Report**
```
You are a product manager, help me write this week's work report, including: 1. Completed requirement reviews 2. Development coordination status 3. User feedback handling 4. Next week plans. Requirements: Clear structure, each point under 50 words, formal tone.
```

**Scenario 2: Writing Monthly Report**
```
You are a marketing specialist, help me write a monthly work summary, including: 1. Main work completed this month 2. Achievements (preferably with data) 3. Existing problems 4. Next month plans. Requirements: Use data, professional formal language, around 800 words.
```

### Step 4: Review and Edit

After AI writes, be sure to check these points:

1. **Fact verification**: Confirm AI's written projects are actually yours
2. **Data verification**: If there's data, must verify accuracy
3. **Tone adjustment**: Adjust tone based on your and boss's habits
4. **Privacy check**: Don't leak sensitive information

## Common Questions

### Q1: What if AI's report is too vague?
A: Provide more specific work content. The more details you give, the more specific AI writes.

### Q2: Can leaders tell it's written by AI at a glance?
A: As long as you review, edit appropriately, generally no one can tell. The key is adding your actual work content.

### Q3: Can I use AI to generate fake work content?
A: Absolutely not! Weekly and monthly reports must be truthful - AI helps organize and express, not fabricate content.

### Q4: What if different companies have different report requirements?
A: Clearly tell AI your company's requirements in the instructions, like "Our company requires concise reports, each point no more than 3 lines."
