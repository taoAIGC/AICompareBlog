## 论文场景的AI组合

### 场景一：文献综述

**输入提示词**：
> "关于'深度学习在医学影像诊断中的应用'，帮我写一段200字的文献综述开场，包含2023-2024的最新进展。"

**ChatGPT版**：
```
In recent years, deep learning has made significant advances in medical image analysis. 
Convolutional neural networks (CNNs) have achieved remarkable performance in tasks 
such as tumor detection, organ segmentation, and disease classification. 
Recent studies have explored attention mechanisms and transformer architectures 
to improve diagnostic accuracy. The development of foundation models has shown 
promising results in handling diverse medical imaging modalities.
```

**Claude版**：
```
The application of deep learning in medical imaging has undergone rapid evolution.
Notably, the integration of attention mechanisms has enhanced model interpretability.
Recent breakthrough studies (2023-2024) demonstrate that foundation models, 
pre-trained on large-scale medical datasets, can achieve human-level performance 
across multiple downstream tasks. Key challenges remain in data privacy and 
generalizability across different healthcare systems.
```

**Perplexity版**：
```
Latest research (2023-2024) highlights:
- Google's Med-PaLM 2 achieves state-of-the-art in radiology reports
- Stanford's Chexpert benchmark improvements
- Transformer-based models for multi-modal medical data
- Challenges: data scarcity, annotation costs, model interpretability
[Sources: Nature Medicine, Lancet Digital Health, arXiv]
```

## 学术使用注意事项

### 注意事项1：AI是辅助，不是替代

AI可以帮助：
- 润色语言
- 检查语法
- 提供思路

AI不能代替：
- 原创研究
- 真实数据
- 个人思考

### 注意事项2：声明AI使用

越来越多的期刊要求声明AI辅助写作。建议在论文末尾注明：

> "The authors used AI tools (ChatGPT/Claude) for language editing and suggestions. The core research and conclusions are the authors' original work."

### 注意事项3：核实AI提供的信息

AI可能产生"幻觉"，提供不存在的文献或数据。**一定要核实**。
