## What is an AI Assistant?

Simply put, an AI assistant is an intelligent tool that understands human language and can answer questions. You can:
- Ask it anything

It's like having a personal assistant available 24/7.

## Tips for First-Time Use

### How You Ask Questions Matters

**Don't do this**:
- "Write something for me" - too vague, AI doesn't know what you want
- "You know, that thing..." - AI can't read your mind

**Do this instead**:
- "Write a job application email for me, applying for a product manager position"
- "Summarize the main points of this paper: [paste content]"
- "Explain quantum computing in simple terms"

### The Magic Formula for Prompting AI

```
Role + Task + Specific Requirements + Output Format
```

**Example**:
- "You are a career mentor, help me write a work summary with these requirements: 1. Include achievements and shortcomings 2. Under 500 words 3. Formal tone"

## Common Use Cases

### Case 1: Writing Content

Have AI help you write articles, emails, social media posts:

**Prompt**:
"Write an article about AI technology adoption, around 800 words, casual and humorous tone, suitable for a WeChat public account"

### Case 2: Organizing Information

Turn messy information into tables:

**Prompt**:
"Organize this meeting notes into a summary list: [paste meeting content]"

### Case 3: Learning Help

Have AI explain concepts and summarize knowledge:

**Prompt**:
"Explain what artificial intelligence is in a way that elementary school students can understand"

### Case 4: Translating Foreign Text

Translate foreign language materials:

**Prompt**:
"Translate this English text into Chinese, keeping professional terminology accurate: [paste English]"

## Common Beginner Questions

### Q1: What if AI gives inaccurate answers?
A: Try rephrasing your question with more context. The more specific your question, the more accurate the answer.

### Q2: Will AI leak my privacy?
A: Try not to input sensitive personal information into AI tools, like ID numbers or bank card numbers.

### Q3: What if AI gives wrong answers?
A: AI isn't perfect. You can politely point out the error and ask it to try again.

### Q4: Is the free version enough?
A: For beginners, the free version is more than enough. Consider paid features after you get familiar.

## Advanced Tips

### 1. Continuous Conversation
Don't just ask one question and leave. Follow up to have AI improve its responses.

### 2. Have AI Play a Role
"You're a programmer with 10 years of experience, help me check this code for issues"

### 3. Give Examples
"Write three sentences in this format: [paste example]"

### 4. Break Down Complex Tasks
Split complex tasks into smaller steps.

3. **Practice more** - The more you use it, the better you'll communicate with AI
