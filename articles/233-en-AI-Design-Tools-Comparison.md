## 详细对比

### 1. Canva——设计界的瑞士军刀

Canva needs no introduction, it is the world’s most popular online design tool. In the past two years, AI functions have become more and more powerful, and it has become a savior for "design novices".
**优点**：
- The number of templates is exploding, including social media pictures, PPT, posters, business cards...all types are available- Rich AI functions: AI writing, AI background generation, AI background removal, AI translation- The operation is extremely simple, drag and drop to complete the design- Team collaboration is convenient, and the enterprise version supports brand management- Multi-platform support, can be used on web pages and apps
**缺点**：
- Limited creative freedom, mainly relying on templates- Insufficient complex design capabilities- Domestic network is unstable- The free version has limited functionality
**适合谁**：设计小白、运营人员、社交媒体运营、小微企业

**我的使用感受**：Canva最大的优势是"快"和"简单"。

Make a social media picture, from choosing a template to changing the text, it can be done in 5 minutes. Its AI background removal function is really easy to use. You can upload a product picture and it will be cut out automatically with just one click, which is more than a little faster than a manual eraser.
But it must be said that Canva is more suitable for "templated design". If you want to create some creative art or unique style, its effect is relatively average. The main purpose is to help you save time, not to help you improve your design level.
The free version is enough, the Pro version is $13 per month, and free for students. Canva is still very popular in educational settings.
---
### 2. Midjourney——艺术感拉满的AI绘图神器

Midjourney is one of the most popular AI drawing tools in the past two years. It is characterized by extremely high generation quality and strong artistic sense, which is especially suitable for scenes that require high-quality visual materials.
**优点**：
- The generation quality is top-notch, with delicate pictures and beautiful colors.- Various art styles, from realistic to abstract, you can hold it- The community is active, you can refer to other people’s tips- Continuously update the version, the effect is getting better and better- Supports cushion pictures, you can use reference pictures to generate similar styles
**缺点**：
- Requires certain prompt word skills- Domestic access requires a special network- The fee is not low, 10-30 US dollars per month- Not available for commercial use (copyright dispute)- Higher learning costs
**适合谁**：设计师、创意工作者、自媒体博主、艺术创作者

**使用感受**：Midjourney的效果是真的"炸"。

It is also about generating a "technological poster", and what Midjourney makes looks better than other tools. That light and shadow, that detail, that texture can be used directly.
But usefulness comes at a price. First of all, you have to be able to write prompt words. This thing looks simple, but there are actually many ways to do it. What weights, parameters, style words... it takes a while to learn. Secondly, domestic network access is really painful and often disconnected.
There's also a big issue: copyright. The commercial use of works generated by Midjourney is risky, and copyright ownership has always been controversial. Think carefully before using it for commercial projects.
In terms of price, the free version has a limited amount, and the standard version is $30 per month. Suitable for professional users who are not short of money and pursue quality.
---
### 3. Figma AI——界面设计的AI革命

Figma is the leader in the field of UI design. The AI ​​functions launched in the past two years have directly changed the way UI designers work.
**优点**：
- Specialized in UI design, particularly professional in generating interfaces, components, and prototypes- Automatic layout and responsive design are easily accomplished- AI can help you write code, and designers can also do front-end work- Powerful brand management function and convenient enterprise collaboration- Rich community resources and mature plug-in ecology
**缺点**：
- Mainly UI/UX design, not suitable for graphic design- The fee is higher, the professional version is $15 per month- Domestic access speed is slow- Steep learning curve- AI functions are relatively new and some are still being tested
**适合谁**：UI/UX设计师、产品经理、前端设计师、互联网公司

**使用感受**：Figma AI是"专业工具"，不是给小白用的。

If you are doing app interfaces, web design, and interactive prototypes, Figma AI is really powerful. Its "generate UI" function can draw a complete interface framework for you by entering a description, saving a lot of time.
There is also a very cool feature: AI generated code. Designers can directly export HTML/CSS, saving the time of communicating back and forth with the front end.
But if you are doing graphic design such as posters, business cards, and PPT, Figma is not suitable. It specializes in UI, and you still need to use tools like Canva for graphic design.
In terms of price, it is free for individuals, the professional version is $15/month, and the enterprise version is more expensive. Suitable for professional designers and teams.
---
### 4. Adobe Firefly——专业设计的AI增强

Adobe Firefly is an AI design tool produced by Adobe. It focuses on "AI-assisted design" and is not completely AI-generated.
**优点**：
- Adobe has a strong ecosystem and seamlessly integrates with PS, AI, and PR.- Practical AI functions: generate fill, expand background, AI translation- Copyright security, worry-free commercial use- Stable quality and high professional recognition- Suitable for complex projects
**缺点**：
- It is not a pure AI generation tool, AI is just an auxiliary- Need to be used with PS and AI- Subscriptions are expensive, $54.99 per month for the full version- General domestic visits- High learning costs
**适合谁**：专业设计师、广告公司、企业设计部门

**使用感受**：Adobe Firefly的正确用法是"AI辅助"，不是"AI替代"。

For example, if you make a poster in PS, Firefly can help you:- Extend image backgrounds (no more cropping)- Smart fill (remove unwanted elements)- Style transfer (applying one style to another image)- AI translation (quickly generate multi-language versions)
These functions are all "auxiliary", and the final design still has to be completed by the designer. So if you are looking forward to "entering text and drawing a picture", Firefly may not be suitable.
But if you are already an Adobe user, Firefly is really great. It's not about disrupting your workflow, it's about making it more efficient.
In terms of price, a separate Firefly subscription is $4.99 per month, and the full Creative Cloud subscription is $54.99 per month. Although the full version is expensive, it is worth the price.
---
### 5. 标小智——国产AI设计工具的扛把子

BiaoXiaoZhi is a domestic AI design tool that has emerged in the past two years. It is characterized by Chinese-friendly and good localization.
**优点**：
- Chinese interface, operating habits are in line with domestic users- Strong ability to understand Chinese prompt words- The template style is Chinese-style, suitable for domestic aesthetics- Domestic access is stable and there are no network problems- Cheap and student friendly- Support e-commerce design and operation materials
**缺点**：
- There is a gap between production quality and international first-line products- Relatively few templates- Limited creative freedom- Low level of internationalization- Community resources are not as good as Canva
**适合谁**：国内运营、电商卖家、学生、小微企业

**使用感受**：标小智最大的优势是"接地气"。

First of all, there are no network problems and domestic access is available instantly. Secondly, my ability to understand and generate Chinese is much stronger. Let me give you an example: the same "New Year's Day Promotional Poster" is generated. The content generated by Biao Xiaozhi is obviously more in line with the domestic e-commerce style, and the promotional elements and New Year atmosphere are just right.
The template style is also more Chinese-style business, there are not so many foreign things, and the made things are comfortable for domestic users.
Another very practical point: Biao Xiaozhi has special e-commerce templates, including mobile phone details page, main image, diamond exhibition... There are various e-commerce material templates, which are particularly easy to use for e-commerce.
In terms of price, there is a free version, and the annual fee for the paid version is only a hundred yuan, which is very cost-effective. Strongly recommended as the first choice for domestic users.
