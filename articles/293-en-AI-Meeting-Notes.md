## Usage Tips

### Tip 1: Set Up in Advance

Turn on recording function before meeting starts, don't fumble for your phone when meeting starts.

### Tip 2: Speak Clearly

Speak clearly at moderate speed, this gives higher recognition accuracy. If people speak simultaneously, recognition quality drops.

### Tip 3: Organize Promptly After Meeting

Organize minutes while the meeting is still fresh, send to relevant people. Easy to forget if you wait too long.

### Tip 4: Double Insurance for Important Meetings

For important meetings, use two tools simultaneously - one as main, one as backup, to prevent accidents.

## Important Notes

### ❌ Don't Do This

1. **Don't Fully Rely on AI** - AI might miss some non-verbal information like expressions, tone
2. **Don't Ignore Privacy** - Pay attention to sensitive content meetings
3. **Don't Use Without Checking** - Always review what AI generates

### ✅ Should Do This

1. **Prepare Before Meeting** - Turn on recording device in advance
2. **Check After Meeting** - Review what AI generates
3. **Mark Important Content** - Highlight key decisions
4. **Distribute Promptly** - Send to relevant people as soon as meeting ends
