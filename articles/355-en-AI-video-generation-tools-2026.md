## 1. Runway Gen-3 - Industry Standard

**Pros**:
- Stable video quality, solid industry reputation
- Full features: generation, editing, extension
- Strong control, precise camera movement control
- Active community, lots of tutorials
- Continuous updates

**Cons**:

- Limited free tier

## Pro Tips: Using AI Video More Efficiently

1. **Be specific with prompts**: Don't just say "a cat running," try "an orange cat running from left to right, background is spring meadow, petals floating with wind, cinematic quality"

2. **Generate image first then video**: Many tools support generating key image first, then converting to video—easier to control final result

3. **Generate in segments**: Long videos break easily, generate in parts then combine

4. **Combine with traditional editing**: AI-generated videos often need post-processing with tools like CapCut, adding subtitles, color grading

5. **Try more, complain less**: AI is evolving fast, today's bad output might be great tomorrow—keep experimenting
