## Vi sao nhieu nguoi vua nghe den backlink la lap tuc bien thanh "may rai link"?

Voi rat nhieu nguoi, backlink SEO co nghia ngay lap tuc la:

- di comment khap noi
- dang link len forum
- nem URL vao cac cong dong ngau nhien
- gui hang loat tin nhan bang cong cu tu dong

Nhin thi co ve rat cham chi. Thuc te thuong khong duoc bao nhieu.

Ly do la phan lon nen tang bay gio hoac khong truyen duoc gia tri dang ke, hoac khong mang ve click that, hoac nhin vao la thay ban chi den de chen link.

Ket qua pho bien thuong roi vao mot trong hai kieu:

1. Lam ra rat nhieu link nhung thu hang gan nhu khong nhuc nhich.
2. Gom duoc mot dong link yeu, va website ngay cang giong spam.

Vi vay, bay gio toi khong hoi "hom nay co the di duoc bao nhieu link?" nua. Toi hoi mot cau khac truoc:

**Ngoai viec lam con so tang len, link nay con mang lai dieu gi?**

Neu cau tra loi chi la "nhin bao cao cho dep hon", thi link do thuong khong dang gia bao nhieu.

## Backlink SEO khong chi de tang so luong lien ket

Ve ban chat, backlink la mot tin hieu gui den cong cu tim kiem:

**Mot website khac co xem ban la nguon dang de nhac den, trich dan hay gioi thieu khong?**

Gia tri that su cua no thuong nam o 3 diem:

1. **Giup trang xay dung do tin cay.**  
   Website moi va trang moi thieu khong chi la noi dung, ma con thieu su tin tuong.

2. **Giup cong cu tim kiem phat hien va hieu trang nhanh hon.**  
   Tin hieu se ro hon neu link den tu mot website cung chu de.

3. **Co the mang ve nguoi dung that co nhu cau that.**  
   Backlink tot khong chi de cho bot doc, ma con dua dung nguoi den dung cho.

Vi the, backlink khong phai la de tha dai mot URL o dau do. No la viec duoc nhac den dung noi, dung ly do.

## Toi thuong tu hoi 4 cau nay truoc khi quyet dinh co nen theo duoi mot backlink hay khong

### 1. Ban than trang do co xung dang de gioi thieu khong?
Neu noi dung con chắp va, hay nang cap trang truoc roi moi nghi den backlink.

### 2. Noi xuat hien lien ket co that su lien quan den chu de cua ban khong?
Mot website ban dao bep dat link ve trang cong cu AI cua ban van la mot link, nhung khong co nghia la no huu ich.

### 3. Link nay co gia tri ngoai SEO khong?
Neu nguoi that nhin thay ma kha nang cao la se khong bam, thi gia tri lau dai cua no thuong khong cao.

### 4. Ban xuat hien o do vi ban huu ich, hay vi ban co tinh chen vao?
Kieu thu nhat de ton tai lau hon. Kieu thu hai de bi xoa hon.

Rat nhieu chien dich backlink that bai khong phai vi nguoi ta khong biet lam link, ma vi **ngay tu dau ho da chon sai cho de bat dau.**

## 5 cach lam backlink nay dang tin hon nhieu so voi viec di de lai link trong phan binh luan

### Cach 1: Tao noi dung du xung dang de duoc trich dan

Day la buoc nhieu nguoi bo qua nhat.

Rat nhieu nguoi muon co backlink, nhung trang cua ho khong co gi de nguoi khac tu nhien muon dan lai.

Nhung dang noi dung de kiem backlink tu nhien hon thuong la:

- bai so sanh co test thuc te
- du lieu duoc tong hop gon gang
- mau mien phi
- danh sach cong cu huu ich
- huong dan tung buoc ro rang

Neu ban viet bai ve tieu de SEO ma chi lap lai nhung loi khuyen chung chung, it ai muon trich dan.
Nhung neu ban co cong thuc dat tieu de dung duoc ngay, danh sach loi thuong gap, va vi du sua that, kha nang duoc dan link se tang len rat nhieu.

Noi ngan gon: **khong phai backlink nao cung phai di xin; co nhung backlink tu lon len khi noi dung du manh.**

---

### Cach 2: Tra loi cau hoi cu the trong cong dong phu hop, roi them link o cho thuc su bo sung thong tin

Khong phai backlink tu cong dong nao cung vo ich. Van de nam o cach ban lam.

Neu ca thong diep cua ban chi la "cau tra loi o day, bam vao di", thi no chang khac gi quang cao.

Nhung neu ban tra loi that ro rang truoc, roi de link nhu mot tai lieu doc them, nguoi ta se de chap nhan hon nhieu.

Nhung noi nhu Reddit, forum chuyen nganh hay cong dong nghe nghiep phu hop hon voi cach nay:

- viet cau tra loi chinh ngay trong bai dang
- chi de link o cho can mo rong them
- trang duoc link phai rat sat voi cau hoi dang noi den

Ngay ca khi gia tri SEO truc tiep cua link khong qua cao, no van co the mang ve luot truy cap that, luu bai va chia se tiep.

---

### Cach 3: Tim cac trang tong hop tai nguyen, cong cu va bo suu tap de duoc dua vao

Rat nhieu website von da co san nhung trang kieu nay:

- cong cu SEO duoc de xuat
- thu muc tai nguyen mien phi
- tong hop huong dan cho nguoi moi
- danh sach cong cu pho bien trong mot linh vuc

Nhung trang nhu vay rat hop de lay backlink, vi ban than chung da duoc tao ra de bo sung tai nguyen huu ich.

Neu noi dung hoac san pham cua ban thuc su co cho dung, thi rat dang de chu dong de xuat.

Toi thuong uu tien hai nhom:

- website ngach rat sat voi chu de cua minh
- nhung trang von da co thoi quen gom tai nguyen, cong cu hoac bai doc them

Diem hay cua dang backlink nay la: **boi canh tu nhien, do lien quan cao, va link thuong song lau hon.**

---

### Cach 4: Viet guest post hoac noi dung hop tac, nhung dung bien no thanh trao doi link may moc

Guest post khong chet. Thu da chet la kieu guest post chat luong thap.

Cach van con hieu qua thuong co dang nhu sau:

- ban thuc su dong gop mot noi dung co ich cho website kia
- chu de phu hop voi doc gia cua ho
- link xuat hien o vi tri hop ly, khong phai doan nao cung chen
- trong tam la hop tac noi dung, khong phai chi cham cham vao mot anchor text

Neu ban xem guest post chi la "de lai cho toi mot link keyword la xong", moi thu se tro nen go ep rat nhanh.

Neu ban xem do la mot lan hop tac noi dung that su va tiep noi mot trang lien quan ve website cua minh mot cach tu nhien, chat luong se khac han.

---

### Cach 5: Tan dung link hong va nhung lan nhac ten thuong hieu ma chua gan link

Rat nhieu nguoi bo qua cach nay, nhung thuc ra no kha hieu qua.

Thuong co hai tinh huong:

- **thay the link hong**: mot trang dang tro den tai nguyen cu khong con hoat dong, va bai cua ban co the thay the
- **nhac den thuong hieu nhung chua gan link**: ai do da nhac den thuong hieu, bai viet hay cong cu cua ban, nhung khong dat link

Ca hai truong hop deu de hon outreach lanh, vi boi canh da co san.

Ban khong phai xin xo mot dieu gi tu tren troi roi xuong. Ban chi dang nhac nhe:

- o day co mot link da hong
- o day da nhac den noi dung cua chung toi, neu can co the them nguon link vao

Chi can giong dieu binh thuong, khong nhu mail gui hang loat, ty le thanh cong thuong khong te.

## Nhung kieu backlink "nhin nhu rat co gang" nay toi thuong tranh

- de link may moc trong comment blog
- dang bai hang loat o forum khong lien quan
- mua goi backlink gia re
- nhoi anchor text chinh xac vao tat ca lien ket
- chi nhin so luong ma bo qua chat luong trang nguon

Ngan han thi trong co ve rat xon xao. Dai han thi rat de thanh tai san rac.

Bay gio, cai bay lon voi nhieu chu website khong phai la "khong di backlink", ma la **bo rat nhieu thoi gian vao nhung backlink gan nhu khong tich luy duoc gia tri.**

## Neu ban muon bat dau lam backlink ngay hom nay, cu di theo 5 buoc nay

### Buoc 1: Chon mot trang thuc su xung dang de day manh
Dung co vua vao la day ca website. Chon 1 den 3 trang quan trong truoc.

### Buoc 2: Liet ke 30 trang hoac website that su lien quan
Luc dau, do lien quan quan trong hon so luong.

### Buoc 3: Chuan bi ly do tiep can khac nhau cho tung nhom muc tieu
Trang tai nguyen, bai dang cong dong, trang bao chi va guest post khong nen dung chung mot kieu noi.

### Buoc 4: Bat dau nho, roi nhin phan hoi
Gui truoc 10 den 20 de xuat chat luong. Xem cai gi duoc hoi dap roi moi mo rong.

### Buoc 5: Ghi lai cach nao thuc su mang ve link va click
Neu khong, ban se ban ron nhieu tuan ma van khong biet cai gi co ket qua.

## Cach toi hay lam hon bay gio: de AI goi y huong tiep can truoc, roi so sanh xem email outreach nao nghe giong nguoi that hon

AI rat hop de ho tro giai doan chuan bi backlink, vi du nhu:

- goi y nhung chu de co the website muc tieu quan tam
- nhap vai viet va phac thao vai kieu email outreach khac nhau
- lam cho loi tu gioi thieu ban than bot cung, bot "ban hang"

Khi muon so sanh nhanh cach dien dat giua cac mo hinh, doi luc toi se dung **AI比一比** de xem cung luc phien ban cua Claude, GPT va DeepSeek.

Khong phai de no gui mail thay toi, ma de xem cach nao giong nguoi that hon, cach nao nghe nhu mau co san, va cach nao de nguoi ta muon doc tiep.

Lam backlink nhieu khi khong thieu tai nguyen. Van de la **cau mo dau da nghe nhu email marketing gui hang loat roi.**

## Ket lai

Cang lam backlink nhieu, toi cang thay ro rang rang backlink SEO khong phai la viec nem URL di khap internet.

Nhung lien ket thuc su hieu qua thuong co chung mot diem:

**truoc het no co ich cho con nguoi, sau do moi co ich cho SEO.**

Vi vay, neu ban sap bat dau lam backlink, dung vọi di dang o khap noi.

Hay quay lai xem:

- trang cua ban co xung dang duoc gioi thieu khong?
- nhung noi ban nham toi co that su lien quan khong?
- cach ban xuat hien co giong dang giup nguoi hay giong dang quang cao?

Khi tra loi ro nhung cau hoi do, backlink se on dinh hon nhieu.
