## 三个AI的真实使用感受

### 1. ChatGPT（GPT-4）

**Entrance**: chat.openai.com

**My experience**:
- The earliest AI used, with a familiar taste
- Fast response and refreshing interface
- The plug-in ecosystem is rich and everything can be connected
- 4o version has strong picture understanding ability
- The free version is enough, the Plus version is $20/month

**advantage**:
- Strong overall ability, no obvious shortcomings
- The most complete plug-ins and ecology
- Reliable online search
- Strong multi-modal capabilities

**shortcoming**:
- Chinese answers are sometimes too "translation-like"
- Occasionally "talk nonsense seriously"
- Domestic access is unstable
- expensive

**Suitable scene**:
- English writing and translation
- Creative content generation
- Q&A where you need to look up information online
- Brainstorming complex issues

**Price**: Free version is enough, Plus version is $20/month

---

### 2. Claude 3.5

**Entrance**: claude.ai

**My experience**:
- My coding skills are really good, and I am particularly comfortable writing Python and JavaScript.
- Strong ability to understand context and handle long articles without stretching them.
- Clean interface, no ads
- Fewer refusals than GPT
- The free version has 20 messages per day, which is enough but not exciting enough

**advantage**:
- Strongest coding ability (personal test)
- Excellent processing of long text
- The answer style is natural and does not sound robotic.
- Anthropic takes a rigorous approach to AI

**shortcoming**:
- The free version has too few credits
- Does not support online search
- Domestic access is not stable either
- No plug-in ecosystem

**Suitable scene**:
- Programming development (strongly recommended)
- Long article writing and editing
- Analysis that requires deep thinking
- Translation work

**Price**: Free version 20 items per day, Pro version $20/month

---

### 3. DeepSeek V3

**Entrance**: chat.deepseek.com

**My experience**:
- A surprising work of domestic AI
- Can understand Chinese very well, and there is no sense of translation
- The free quota is generous and basically unlimited
- Fast response
- Reasoning ability has been significantly improved
- The key is - domestic access is stable!

**advantage**:
- Excellent Chinese language ability
- Totally free (for now)
- Domestic access is stable
- Cheap (API is cheaper than GPT)
- Inference model R1 is available for free

**shortcoming**:
- English writing is still a bit Chinglish
- Slightly weaker than GPT in terms of creativity
- Ecology is still under construction

**Suitable scene**:
- Chinese writing and conversation
- Daily Q&A and work assistance
- Introduction and learning of programming
- The first choice for money savers

**Price**: Free! API prices are also very low

## 我的真实选择

**Write code → Claude**
There is no opponent. Claude really understands debugging code, interpreting code, and optimizing code.

**Write articles/produce content → GPT-4**
It has many creative ideas and smooth expression, and is suitable for scenes that require "beautifully written".

**Save money/daily use → DeepSeek**
It costs nothing and the country is stable. What kind of bicycle do you want?
