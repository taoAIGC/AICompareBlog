## Detailed Comparison

### 1. Notion AI - The All-Round Office Tool

Notion is a popular note tool, plus AI, even more powerful.

**Pros**:
- Strong AI writing
- Multiple features integrated
- Easy team collaboration
- Rich templates
- Cross-platform support

**Cons**:
- Requires VPN
- Limited free AI
- Paid version pricey

**Who it's for**: Teams, unified workspace

### 2. Obsidian AI - The Geek's Choice

Obsidian is a local-first note software.

**Pros**:
- Local storage secure
- Powerful bidirectional links
- Rich plugin ecosystem
- AI plugins optional

**Cons**:
- Learning curve
- Needs configuration

**Who it's for**: Privacy-focused users

### 3. Logseq AI - The Thinker's Tool

Logseq is an outline-based note tool.

**Pros**:
- Clear outline mode
- Free and open source
- Local storage
- No VPN needed

**Cons**:
- Simple interface

**Who it's for**: Writers, privacy users

---

## Conclusion

- Notion AI: Best for collaboration
- Obsidian AI: Best for privacy
- Logseq AI: Best free option
