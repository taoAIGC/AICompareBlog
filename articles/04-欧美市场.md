## Each AI Has Unique Strengths

Think of each AI as a different expert consultant:

### ChatGPT: The Generalist
- **Best for**: Quick answers, creative tasks, general knowledge
- **Strengths**: Fast, reliable, massive knowledge base
- **Weaknesses**: May lack depth in specialized topics

### Claude: The Analyst
- **Best for**: Code review, security analysis, reasoning
- **Strengths**: Deep analysis, safety-focused, explains "why"
- **Weaknesses**: Slightly slower responses

### Perplexity: The Researcher
- **Best for**: Finding latest information, fact-checking
- **Strengths**: Real-time web search, citations included
- **Weaknesses**: May lack creative thinking

### Gemini: The Integrator
- **Best for**: Google's ecosystem, multimodal tasks
- **Strengths**: Works well with Google services, images
- **Weaknesses**: Still maturing in some areas
